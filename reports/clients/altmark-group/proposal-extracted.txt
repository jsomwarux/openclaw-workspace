SECURITY
All data stays on your hardware. The automation server runs on your network, on your machines.
Tenant data, financial records, and documents do not leave your building.
You own everything. No vendor lock-in, no SaaS dependency. If we part ways, every workflow and
configuration remains yours and keeps running.
AI calls are controlled and sanitized. When AI processing is needed, only the minimum necessary data
is sent. Tenant PII is stripped where possible. Enterprise zero-data-retention API agreements are
available.
Human-in-the-loop on sensitive actions. No workflow takes financial action without human approval.
The system flags and recommends — your team decides.
Full audit trail. Every action by every workflow is logged. Complete visibility into what ran, when, and
what it did.
TIMELINE
Everything starts with the Navid infrastructure session — that's where we confirm the integration approach and lock
in all timelines. Foundation + first use case can be live within 2 weeks of that session. Full buildout across all six
use cases: 8–10 weeks.
