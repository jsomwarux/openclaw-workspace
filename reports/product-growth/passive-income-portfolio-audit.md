## Product-by-product notes

- Target user: crypto retail user who wants higher-signal token discovery without trusting one influencer or one model.
- Core promise: 4-LLM consensus rankings and early alerts on high-consensus picks.
- Monetization path: paid alerts / watchlists / pro rankings.
- Distribution loop: SEO/AI crawler pages + TikTok slideshow loop + X proof posts.
- Maintenance burden: medium, but justified if ranking pages compound organic traffic.
- Kill/pause threshold: pause feature work if pricing-click or alert-signup intent stays flat after SEO pages ship.
