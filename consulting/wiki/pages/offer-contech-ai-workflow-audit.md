## Summary
A 7-day review of one construction field workflow from intake → handoff → update → exception escalation.

## Buyer promise
Turn a messy jobsite/office workflow into an AI-ready pilot plan that can be implemented safely.

## Deliverables
- workflow map
- AI-fit opportunities
- risk/approval points
- sample automation or agent spec
- rollout checklist

## Best-fit buyer
Construction, contech, real estate operations, or field-heavy teams that already know AI matters but have not mapped one workflow tightly enough for implementation.

## Positioning
Use niche language, not generic AI enablement. The pitch is not “AI strategy.” It is implementation prep for one workflow.

Good line:
> The hard part is not picking tools. It is mapping field workflows cleanly enough that AI can survive contact with the jobsite.

## Architecture overlay
Use [[concept-supervisor-specialist-judge-human]] when scoping the workflow:
- supervisor: route jobsite input/request type
- specialists: retrieve docs/photos/status, draft updates, check structured fields
- judge: validate confidence, missing info, risk, approval need
- human: approve exceptions, override, own customer/vendor update
