# High-Stakes Draft Eval

Use this before shipping important copy. The goal is to catch generic language, stale evidence, unsupported claims, weak CTAs, and ICP mismatch.

## Required checks
1. **Specificity:** Does the draft include concrete signals, names, workflows, roles, metrics, or source-backed facts?
2. **Source freshness:** Are claims based on current evidence, not stale cached text?
3. **ICP fit:** Does it speak to the buyer’s operational world instead of generic AI interest?
4. **Proof:** Does it include a believable proof point or credible reason to listen?
5. **CTA:** Is the ask low-friction and open-ended?
6. **Voice:** No corporate filler, fake enthusiasm, em dashes in outreach copy, or “AI consulting agency” framing.
7. **Risk:** No overclaiming, no replacing-the-FTE framing, no confidential/client-sensitive details.

## Verdict format
Return one of:
- `SHIP` — ready.
- `FIX THEN SHIP` — list exact edits.
- `HOLD` — source/positioning risk is material.

## Optional model council
Use only for important external-facing assets or repeated failures:
- one critic pass for genericness/specificity
- one precision pass for factual/source risk

Do not run expensive model councils for routine drafts.
