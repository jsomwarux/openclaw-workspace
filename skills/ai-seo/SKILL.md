## How AI Search Works

| Platform | Source Selection |
|----------|-----------------|
| Google AI Overviews | Strong correlation with traditional rankings — fix SEO first |
| ChatGPT (with search) | Draws from wider range, not just top-ranked |
| Perplexity | Always cites sources — favors authoritative, recent, well-structured |
| Gemini | Google index + Knowledge Graph |
| Claude | Training data + Brave search results |

**Key difference from traditional SEO:** Traditional SEO gets you ranked. AI SEO gets you **cited**.

AI systems look for:
1. **Authoritative answers** to specific questions
2. **Structured, extractable content** (clear headings, lists, direct answers)
3. **Entity clarity** — is it unambiguous who/what you are?
4. **Citation worthiness** — would a human researcher cite this source?

## The GEO Optimization Framework

### Tier 1: Foundation (must have before anything else)
- [ ] **llms.txt** — tells AI crawlers how to understand your site. Already done for jtsomwaru.com.
- [ ] **JSON-LD schema** — Person, Organization, Service, FAQPage schemas. Already done for jtsomwaru.com.
- [ ] **Open AI crawler access** — robots.txt allows GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot. Already done.
- [ ] **Clean canonical URLs** — no duplicate content issues
- [ ] **Page speed** — slow pages get deprioritized

### Tier 2: Content Structure
AI systems extract answers. Structure content so the answer is obvious:

**Direct answer format (what Perplexity loves):**
```
## What is [topic]?
[Direct 1-2 sentence answer]

[Expanded explanation in short paragraphs]

[Bullet points for key facts]
```

**FAQ sections** — literally answer the question in the heading, then answer it in the first sentence of the body.

**Definition blocks** — for any technical term or service you offer, define it clearly in one sentence.

### Tier 3: Entity Establishment
AI systems need to understand who JT is and what he does:
- Consistent Name + Location + Specialty across all online profiles
- Wikipedia-style clear first paragraph: "Jon Trevor Somwaru is an AI consultant based in NYC specializing in n8n workflow automation and Salesforce Agentforce implementation for small businesses."
- LinkedIn, X bio, jtsomwaru.com About section — all should describe the same person the same way

### Tier 4: Citation Signals
Being cited by others trains AI systems to cite you:
- Guest posts and mentions on industry sites
- Being referenced in subreddit discussions
- Directory listings (Futurepedia, AlternativeTo, G2 for consulting tools)
- LinkedIn articles that get shared and linked

## Content Formats That Get Cited

**High citation probability:**
1. **Definitive guides** — "The Complete Guide to n8n for Small Business" (long, comprehensive, regularly updated)
2. **Original data/research** — "I analyzed 50 NYC SMB automation projects — here's what I found"
3. **Comparison content** — "n8n vs Make.com for construction companies" (comparative, specific)
4. **Process documentation** — "How to set up Agentforce for property management" (step-by-step)
5. **FAQ pages** — answer 20+ real questions people ask in your niche

**Low citation probability:**
- Generic marketing copy
- Content that could apply to anyone
- Vague claims without specifics
- Content without a clear author/entity
