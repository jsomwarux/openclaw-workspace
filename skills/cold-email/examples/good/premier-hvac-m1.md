# Good Example: Premier HVAC — M1
**Niche:** Construction / HVAC
**Opener format:** Curiosity opener (peer-level question)
**Personalization level:** MEDIUM — company scale signal (4 boroughs, 7 days), no individual post found
**Individuation note:** No individual-specific signal found — used operational scale as proxy

---

Subject: dispatch setup

Boris — running HVAC across four boroughs seven days a week is no small thing. Curious: is your job coordination still going through one dispatcher, or have you built anything around that yet?

Been working on exactly this with a similar multi-borough contractor.

---

**Why this works:**
- Opens with an acknowledgment of scale that signals real research (4 boroughs, 7 days)
- The question is genuinely open-ended — it doesn't assume their pain, it invites them to confirm it
- "Been working on exactly this" is the proof point signal without a full pitch
- Under 40 words. Hard to ignore.
- No em dashes, no subject line that reads like a slide title, no signature block
