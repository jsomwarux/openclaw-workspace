## Capture Prompts
After each delivery block, ask and answer:
1. What edge case, objection, field-mapping issue, approval gate, or handoff failure would repeat at another client?
2. What part can become a checklist, template, workflow node, demo, content angle, or sales proof?
3. What client-specific/private detail must be removed before reuse?
4. What evidence is still missing before this can be used externally?

Promotion rule: reusable IP is not “ready” until it has a next asset, owner, privacy boundary, and evidence status.
