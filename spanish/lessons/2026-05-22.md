# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-22
**Curriculum week used:** Week 12
**Topic:** Expressing varying needs

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **No tengo ganas de...** (I don't feel like...)
2. **Me urge...** (It's urgent for me / I really need to...)
3. **Me hace falta...** (I'm missing / I need...)
4. **Preferiría...** (I would prefer...)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. Your girlfriend suggests going to a loud club. You're tired. Tell her: "I don't feel like going out, I would prefer to stay home."
2. You realize you don't have enough cash for a tip. Tell her: "I need (I'm missing) five dollars."

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Today, use 'No tengo ganas de...' or 'Preferiría...' at least once when making a decision about food or plans.
