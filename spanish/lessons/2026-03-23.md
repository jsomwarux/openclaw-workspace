# Lesson — 2026-03-23 (Day 19)
**Week:** 4 | **Topic:** conocer vs. saber
**Streak:** 14 | **Level:** A1+

## Phrases Covered
1. **Sé** — "I know" (fact/information) | SAY
2. **Conozco** — "I know [a person/place]" | koh-NOHS-koh
3. **¿Sabes...?** — "Do you know...?" (asking about a fact) | SAH-behs
4. **¿Conoces...?** — "Do you know...?" (asking about a person/place) | koh-NOH-sehs

## Key Rule
- **Saber** = knowing facts, information, how to do something
- **Conocer** = knowing/being familiar with a person or place

## Example Sentences
- "No sé adónde vamos esta noche." — I don't know where we're going tonight.
- "Conozco a su mamá — es muy amable." — I know her mom — she's very kind.
- "¿Sabes dónde está el baño?" — Do you know where the bathroom is?
- "¿Conoces un buen restaurante por aquí?" — Do you know a good restaurant around here?

## Practice Prompts
1. Ask if she knows whether the restaurant takes reservations → ¿Sabes si...
2. Say you don't know that person → No conozco a...
