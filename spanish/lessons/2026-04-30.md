# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-30
**Week 9, Day 4 | Topic: Shopping & Buying Things**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Cuánto cuesta?** — How much does it cost?
2. **¿Puedo probármelo?** — Can I try it on?
3. **Solo estoy mirando.** — I'm just looking.
4. **Te queda bien.** — It looks good on you. / It fits you well.

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around
- "¿Cuánto cuesta?" = "how much does it cost?" — the essential shopping question
- "¿Puedo probármelo?" = "can I try it on myself?" — reflexive, very natural in stores
- "Te queda bien" = "it remains well on you" = it fits/looks good — what you say to your girlfriend in the fitting room
- Memory hook: "Probar" = to try/test — "probármelo" = try it on me. The -melo combines me + lo.
