# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-18
**Curriculum week used:** Week 12
**Topic:** Coordination: "I'm busy" vs. "I'm free"

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **Estoy ocupado / Estoy libre** (I'm busy / I'm free)
2. **Avísame cuando [estés lista/termines]** (Let me know when [you're ready/you finish])
3. **Ya casi llego** (I'm almost there)
4. **Espérame** (Wait for me)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. Your girlfriend asks if you can talk now. Reply that you're busy but to let you know when she finishes work.
2. You're meeting her at a restaurant and you're 2 blocks away. Tell her you're almost there and to wait for you.

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Use "ya casi llego" or "avísame cuando..." via text or voice with your girlfriend today during your normal coordination.
