# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-13
**Week 7, Day 1 | Topic: Starting Conversations + Small Talk**

## Key Phrases
1. **Hola, ¿qué tal tu día?** — Hi, how was your day?
2. **¿Qué has hecho hoy?** — What have you done today?
3. **Nada nuevo, ¿y tú?** — Nothing new, and you?
4. **Me alegra verte.** — I'm glad to see you.

## Notes
- Week 7 theme: Social Conversations
- Day 1: conversation openers and small talk — bridging into deeper topics
- Week 6 ended with emotional vocabulary (reacting to feelings/news). Week 7 starts with HOW to open and sustain a conversation naturally.
- Strong: past tense (fui, comí, estuve) — use naturally in "¿Qué has hecho?"
