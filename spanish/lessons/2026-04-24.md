# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-24
**Week 8, Day 5 | Topic: Checking In & Showing You Care**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Cómo te fue?** — How did it go? / How was your day?
2. **Estoy pensando en ti.** — I'm thinking about you.
3. **¿Ya llegaste bien?** — Did you get there OK?
4. **Cuéntame.** — Tell me. / Fill me in.

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life — last day of new content before evaluation
- "¿Cómo te fue?" = literally "how did it go for you?" — the natural "how was your day?"
- "Cuéntame" = imperative of contar (to tell) — casual, warm way to say "tell me what's up"
- "¿Ya llegaste bien?" = ya (already) + llegaste (you arrived) + bien (well) — checking they got somewhere safe
- Memory hook: "Cuéntame" = "count me in" on your story — tell me everything
