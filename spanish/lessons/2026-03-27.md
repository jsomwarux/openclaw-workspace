# Lesson — 2026-03-27 (Day 22, Week 4)
**Topic:** Mixed tense conversation flow — combining present + past naturally
**Streak:** 15 days

## Phrases Covered
1. **Hoy estoy cansado porque ayer salí tarde** — "Today I'm tired because I went out late yesterday" (present + past in one sentence)
2. **Fui a [place] y estuvo increíble** — "I went to [place] and it was incredible"
3. **La semana pasada... y ahora...** — "Last week... and now..." (bridging past to present)
4. **Antes no sabía, pero ya aprendí** — "Before I didn't know, but now I've learned"

## Review Questions (from Day 20 — past tense + transitions)
1. ¿Cómo se dice "I ate"? → Comí
2. ¿Cómo se dice "I went"? → Fui
3. Translate: "I saw a movie and after that I went home" → Vi una película y después fui a casa

## Practice Prompts
1. Tell me about something you did this week using past + a reaction in present ("Fui a... y ahora...")
2. Your girlfriend asks: "¿Cómo estuvo tu semana?" — answer with 2 things that happened + how you feel now

## Challenge (Level 2)
Order your full coffee order at a café in Spanish — say the size, the drink, and say "por favor" and "gracias."
