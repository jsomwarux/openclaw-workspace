# Lesson — 2026-03-24 (Day 20, Week 4)
**Topic:** Past tense drill + natural transitions
**Streak:** 15 days

## Phrases Covered
1. **Comí** — I ate (past of comer)
2. **Fui** — I went (past of ir)
3. **Vi** — I saw / I watched (past of ver)
4. **Después / luego / y entonces** — after that / then / and then (transition connectors)

## Review Questions (from Day 19 — conocer vs saber)
1. "I know this neighborhood well" → Conozco bien este vecindario
2. "I know how to drive" → Sé manejar
3. ¿Sabes dónde está el restaurante? → Do you know where the restaurant is?

## Practice Prompts
1. ¿Qué hiciste hoy? — answer with 2 things + one transition word
2. Tell me about something you watched recently using *vi*

## Challenge (Level 2)
Order coffee entirely in Spanish: "Quisiera un café, por favor" or "Me da un café con leche, por favor."

## TTS Sent
Comí. Fui. Vi. Después… luego… y entonces.
