# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-21
**Curriculum week used:** Week 12
**Topic:** Asking for specific favors

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **¿Puedes traerme...?** (Can you bring me...?)
2. **Me haces un favor** (Do me a favor / You do me a favor)
3. **Tráeme...** (Bring me... [command])
4. **Llévate...** (Take [with you]...)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. You're on the couch and realize your phone is in the other room. Ask your girlfriend: "Do me a favor, can you bring me my phone?"
2. She's leaving the house to go to the store. Tell her: "Take the umbrella (el paraguas), it's raining."

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Today, ask your girlfriend for one small thing using "¿Puedes traerme...?" (a drink, a charger, a snack).
