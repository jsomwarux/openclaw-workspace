# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-14
**Week 7, Day 2 | Topic: Wishes & Desires**

## Key Phrases
1. **Quisiera hacer algo especial esta noche.** — I'd love to do something special tonight.
2. **¿Qué quieres hacer mañana?** — What do you want to do tomorrow?
3. **Me encantaría conocerte mejor.** — I'd love to get to know you better.
4. **¿Te gustaría salir esta semana?** — Would you like to go out this week?

## Notes
- Week 7 theme: Social Conversations — greetings, preferences, asking for help, expressing enthusiasm
- Wishes & Desires bridges from Week 6 emotional vocabulary into active planning/making invitations
- Memory hook: "Quisiera" = I would like (past subjunctive = softer, more polite than "quiero")
- Strong areas: polite ordering with "quisiera" already established — this extends it naturally
