# Lesson — 2026-03-30 (Day 24, Week 5)
**Topic:** Wants, needs, and intentions — quiero, necesito, tengo que, voy a
**Streak:** 17 days

## Week 5 Theme
Expressing yourself more fully — what you want, need, plan to do. Moves from reactive (answering questions) to proactive (initiating conversation).

## Phrases Covered
1. **Quiero...** — "I want..." (quiero comer, quiero ir, quiero un café)
2. **Necesito...** — "I need..." (necesito más tiempo, necesito un vaso de agua)
3. **Tengo que...** — "I have to / I need to..." (tengo que trabajar, tengo que irme)
4. **Voy a...** — "I'm going to..." (voy a comer, voy a llamarte, voy a ir contigo)

## Review Questions (from Day 23 — family dinner simulation)
1. ¿Cómo se dice "Thanks for having me"? → Gracias por invitarme
2. ¿Cómo se dice "Everything tastes so good!"? → ¡Qué rico está todo!
3. ¿Cómo se dice "It was a pleasure meeting you all"? → Fue un placer conocerlos

## Practice Prompts
1. You're at dinner and want to order — tell the waiter what you want to eat and drink
2. Your girlfriend asks if you want to go out tonight — say yes, you want to go, but you have to finish work first

## Challenge (Level 2)
Order your full coffee order at a café in Spanish — say the size, the drink, and say "por favor" and "gracias."
