# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-28
**Week 9, Day 1 | Topic: Getting Around & Directions**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Dónde queda...?** — Where is...? / Where is... located?
2. **Estamos cerca.** — We're close.
3. **¿Está lejos?** — Is it far?
4. **Vamos en taxi / Uber.** — Let's take a taxi / Uber.

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around
- "¿Dónde queda...?" = "where does it remain/sit?" — the natural way to ask where something is located, more common than "¿dónde está?" for places
- "Estamos cerca" = "we are close" — reassuring when navigating together
- "¿Está lejos?" = "is it far?" — essential for deciding transportation
- Memory hook: "Queda" = "remains/sits" — think "where does it sit on the map?"
