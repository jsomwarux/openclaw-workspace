# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-16
**Curriculum week used:** Week 11
**Topic:** Full dinner simulation (Arrive + Compliment + Question + Story)

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **Bienvenidos / Gracias por la invitación** (Welcome / Thanks for the invitation)
2. **Qué rico todo** (Everything is so delicious)
3. **Pásame el agua, por favor** (Pass me the water, please)
4. **Provecho** (Enjoy your meal - said when starting or leaving the table)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. You just arrived at the house. Thank them for the invitation and tell them everything looks/smells delicious.
2. During the meal, ask someone to pass the water and then use 'Provecho' naturally.

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Perform the full sequence tonight with your girlfriend: greet, compliment her cooking (or the food), ask one follow-up question about her day, and say 'provecho'.
