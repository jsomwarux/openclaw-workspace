# Lesson 26 — April 2, 2026
**Week:** 5 | **Day:** 26 | **Topic:** Talking About the Future + Making Plans

## Phrases Covered

| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---|---|---|
| Voy a... | BOY ah | I'm going to... |
| ¿Quieres ir a...? | KYEH-res EER ah | Do you want to go to...? |
| Tengo planes. / No tengo planes. | TEN-goh PLAH-nes | I have plans. / I don't have plans. |
| Va a ser divertido. | bah ah SEHR dee-vehr-TEE-doh | It's going to be fun. |

## Example Sentences Used
- "Voy a cocinar esta noche."
- "¿Quieres ir a comer algo después?"
- "Este fin de semana no tengo planes — ¿qué quieres hacer?"
- "Va a ser divertido — nunca fui a ese lugar."

## Review (from Day 25 — opinions/agreeing/disagreeing)
1. "I think the food is good" → *Creo que la comida está buena*
2. "You're right" → *Tienes razón*
3. "I disagree — I prefer this restaurant" → *No estoy de acuerdo — prefiero este restaurante*

## Practice Prompts
1. Tell girlfriend plans for this weekend using voy a + va a ser divertido
2. Respond to movie invitation — say yes + ask what time

## Challenge (Level 2)
Tell girlfriend one real plan tonight using *voy a*.
