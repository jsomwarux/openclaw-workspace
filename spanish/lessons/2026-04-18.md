# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-18
**Week 7, Day 5 | Topic: Expressing Enthusiasm**

## Key Phrases
1. **¡Qué chimba!** — That's awesome! / Cool! (Colombia/Mexico slang)
2. **¡Está increíble!** — It's amazing!
3. **¡Me encanta!** — I love it!
4. **¡Qué padre!** — How cool! (Mexico)

## Notes
- Week 7 theme: Social Conversations — last day
- These let you react with genuine energy — not just "sí" or "está bien"
- Strong areas: "me encantaría" already in vocabulary — this is the casual form
- Memory hook: "Chimba" = Colombian slang for "awesome" — fun to say | "Padre" = father → "that's father-level cool" = amazing
