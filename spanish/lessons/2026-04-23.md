# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-23
**Week 8, Day 4 | Topic: Saying Goodbye & Parting Phrases**

## Key Phrases
1. **Nos vemos pronto.** — See you soon.
2. **Cuídate mucho.** — Take good care of yourself.
3. **Que descanses.** — Sleep well. / Rest well.
4. **Hablamos mañana.** — We'll talk tomorrow.

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life
- "Que descanses" = subjunctive, literally "may you rest" — the standard goodnight phrase
- "Hablamos mañana" = "we talk tomorrow" — natural way to end a call/conversation
- "Cuídate" = cuidar (to take care) + te — softer than just "adiós"
- Memory hook: "Que descanses" = think "que descanses bien" (rest well) — the "que" is like a wish
