# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-17
**Week 7, Day 4 | Topic: Filler Words & Natural Connectors**

## Key Phrases
1. **Pues...** — Well... / So...
2. **Mira...** — Look... / See...
3. **Oye...** — Hey... / Listen...
4. **O sea...** — I mean... / Like...

## Notes
- Week 7 theme: Social Conversations
- These are the "umms" and "wells" of natural Spanish — they buy you thinking time AND signal you're about to say something real
- Strong areas: filler words introduced in Week 3 but need deep contextual practice IN sentences, not isolated
- Memory hook: "Mira" = "look" (literally) — you're pointing attention | "Oye" = "oyé" with an ey-sound, like you're catching someone's ear | "Pues" = like "well" in English | "O sea" = "or rather" — you're about to rephrase
