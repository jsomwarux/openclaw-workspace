# Spanish Lesson — 2026-05-02
**Week 9, Day 6 | Topic: Travel Wrap-Up & Combo Phrases**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Cómo llego a...?** — How do I get to...?
2. **¿Está incluido?** — Is it included?
3. **¿Aceptan tarjeta?** — Do you take card?
4. **Disculpe, ¿me puede ayudar?** — Excuse me, can you help me?

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around — wrap-up day
- "¿Cómo llego a...?" = "how do I arrive at...?" — asking for directions, more natural than "¿dónde está?" when you want routing
- "¿Está incluido?" = "is it included?" — breakfast, taxes, fees — always ask
- "¿Aceptan tarjeta?" = "do you accept card?" — essential in cash-heavy countries
- "Disculpe, ¿me puede ayudar?" = polite way to start any request for help
- Memory hook: "Llego" = I arrive — think "lego my ego at the door, I just need to get there"
