# Spanish Lesson — March 3, 2026
**Week:** 1 | **Day:** 2 | **Topic:** Feelings & States

## Phrases Covered
1. **¿Cómo estás?** — "How are you?" (standard casual greeting follow-up)
2. **Estoy cansado.** — "I'm tired." (male speaker)
3. **Estoy contento.** — "I'm happy / in a good mood." (male speaker; contenta for female)
4. **Estoy ocupado.** — "I'm busy."

## Review Prompts (from Day 1 — Greetings)
- ¿Cómo se dice "Nice to meet you"? → Mucho gusto
- ¿Cómo se dice "What's your name"? → ¿Cómo te llamas?
- Translate: "Encantado, ¿y tú?" → "Pleased to meet you, and you?"

## Practice Prompts Given
- "Your girlfriend texts: ¿Cómo estás? — long day. What do you say?"
- "At her family's dinner, her mom asks how you are. You're in a great mood — reply."

## Real-World Challenge
Level 1 — Say "buenos días" or "buenas tardes" to someone in public. Bonus: follow with ¿Cómo estás?
