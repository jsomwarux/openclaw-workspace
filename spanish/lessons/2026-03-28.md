# Lesson — 2026-03-28 (Day 23, Week 4)
**Topic:** Family dinner simulation — arrival, introductions, food, small talk
**Streak:** 16 days

## Phrases Covered
1. **Gracias por invitarme** — "Thanks for having me / Thanks for the invite"
2. **¡Qué rico está todo!** — "Everything tastes so good!" (at the table)
3. **¿Me puede pasar...?** — "Can you pass me...?" (polite table request)
4. **Fue un placer conocerlos** — "It was a pleasure meeting you all" (on the way out)

## Review Questions (from Day 22 — mixed tense flow)
1. Translate: "Today I'm tired because I went out late yesterday" → Hoy estoy cansado porque ayer salí tarde
2. ¿Cómo se dice "Last week... and now..."? → La semana pasada... y ahora...
3. ¿Cómo se dice "I went there and it was incredible"? → Fui ahí y estuvo increíble

## Practice Prompts
1. You just arrived at your GF's family dinner — greet everyone and say thanks for having you
2. Dinner is winding down — compliment the food and say it was a pleasure meeting everyone

## Challenge (Level 2)
Order your full coffee order at a café in Spanish — say the size, the drink, and say "por favor" and "gracias."
