# Lesson 10 — Friday, March 13, 2026
**Week 2 | Topic: Making Plans**

## Phrases Covered

| Phrase | Pronunciation | Meaning |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| ¿Quieres ir? | KYEH-res EER | Do you want to go? |
| Vamos a... | BAH-mos ah | Let's go to... / We're going to... |
| ¿A qué hora? | ah KEH OH-rah | At what time? |
| Esta noche | EHS-tah NOH-cheh | Tonight |

## Example Sentences
- ¿Quieres ir a cenar esta noche? — Do you want to go eat dinner tonight?
- Vamos a ese restaurante que te gusta. — Let's go to that restaurant you like.
- ¿A qué hora quieres salir? — What time do you want to leave?
- ¿Qué quieres hacer esta noche? — What do you want to do tonight?
