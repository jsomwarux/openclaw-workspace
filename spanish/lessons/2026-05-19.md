# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-19
**Curriculum week used:** Week 12
**Topic:** Nuanced reactions (surprise/doubt)

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **¡No me digas!** (No way! / Don't tell me!)
2. **¿En serio?** (Seriously?)
3. **No lo puedo creer** (I can't believe it)
4. **Me parece raro** (It seems strange to me)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. Your girlfriend tells you her dog just started talking. React with "No way!" and ask "Seriously?".
2. A coworker says the office is closing for a month. Tell them you can't believe it and that it seems strange.

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Use '¿En serio?' or '¡No me digas!' today when someone tells you something interesting or surprising.
