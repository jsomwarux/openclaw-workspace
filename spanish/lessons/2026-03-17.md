# Lesson 13 — Talking About the Past
**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Week:** 3 | **Day:** 13
**Topic:** Talking About the Past (preterite — first-person key verbs)

## Phrases Covered

| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| comí | koh-MEE | I ate |
| fui | FWEE | I went |
| vi | BEE | I saw / I watched |
| estuvo bien | ehs-TOO-boh BIEHN | It was good |
| fue divertido | FWEH dee-behr-TEE-doh | It was fun |

## Example Sentences
- *Comí pizza con mi novia anoche.* — I ate pizza with my girlfriend last night.
- *Fui al gimnasio esta mañana.* — I went to the gym this morning.
- *Vi una película buenísima anoche.* — I watched an amazing movie last night.
- *Fui a un restaurante — estuvo bien.* — I went to a restaurant — it was good.
- *Fue divertido, la verdad.* — It was fun, honestly.

## Practice Prompts
1. GF asks ¿Qué hiciste hoy? — describe one thing you did
2. GF asks how it was — estuvo bien or fue divertido
