## Key Phrases Covered
1. **once, doce, trece, catorce, quince, dieciséis, diecisiete, dieciocho, diecinueve, veinte** — Numbers 11–20
2. **treinta, cuarenta, cincuenta, sesenta, setenta, ochenta, noventa, cien** — Tens 30–100
3. **¿Cuánto cuesta?** — How much does it cost?
4. **Me da...** — Can I get... / Give me... (at a counter or restaurant)
