# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-20
**Week 8, Day 1 | Topic: Daily Routines & Home Life**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Qué vas a hacer hoy?** — What are you going to do today?
2. **Acabo de llegar.** — I just got home / I just arrived.
3. **Estoy cansado/a, pero contento/a.** — I'm tired, but happy.
4. **¿Comiste algo?** — Did you eat anything?

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life — routines, check-ins, casual domestic conversation
- "Acabo de" = "I just ___" — incredibly useful connector phrase (acabo de comer, acabo de hablar, etc.)
- "Cansado/a" — the -o is masculine, -a is feminine. JT uses -o.
- Memory hook: "Acabo" sounds like "a cabo" = "to the end" — you just finished doing something
