# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-21
**Week 8, Day 2 | Topic: Making Plans**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Qué hacemos esta noche?** — What are we doing tonight?
2. **¿Te parece si...?** — How about if...? / What if we...?
3. **Quedamos a las ocho.** — Let's meet at eight. / We'll meet at eight.
4. **Avísame cuando llegues.** — Let me know when you arrive.

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life
- "¿Te parece si...?" = literally "Does it seem OK to you if...?" — natural way to float a suggestion without being pushy
- "Quedamos" = we agree to meet/hang — very natural for making casual plans
- "Avísame" = imperative of "avisar" (to notify) + me — "notify me" = let me know
- Memory hook: "Avísame" sounds like "advise me" — you're asking them to advise you of their arrival
