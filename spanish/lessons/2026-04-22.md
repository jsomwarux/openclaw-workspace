# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-22
**Week 8, Day 3 | Topic: Talking About Food & Eating Together**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Qué se te antoja?** — What are you craving? / What sounds good to you?
2. **Está buenísimo.** — It's really good. / It's delicious.
3. **¿Probaste el/la...?** — Did you try the...?
4. **Pídelo, te va a gustar.** — Order it, you're going to like it.

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life
- "¿Qué se te antoja?" = THE natural phrase for "what do you feel like eating?" — not "¿qué quieres comer?" which is more blunt
- "Buenísimo" = bueno + -ísimo (superlative intensifier) — same pattern as "delicioso" but more casual
- "Pídelo" = imperative of pedir (to order) + lo — "order it"
- Memory hook: "Antojo" = craving — "what's calling to you?" vibe
