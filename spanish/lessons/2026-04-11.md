## Theme
Week 6 brought you: Emotions, Agreeing/Disagreeing, Describing Feelings, and Reacting to Good News. Today: put it all together in one flowing conversation at the dinner table.

## Key Phrases From This Week
1. **¡Qué emoción!** — How exciting!
2. **¡Estoy de acuerdo!** / **No estoy seguro.** — I agree! / I'm not sure.
3. **Me siento...** — I feel...
4. **¡Qué buena noticia!** / **Me alegro mucho por ti.** — What great news! / I'm so happy for you.
