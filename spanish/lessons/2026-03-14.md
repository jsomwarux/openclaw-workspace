## Phrases Covered

| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| Fui al / Fui a | FOO-ee al / FOO-ee ah | I went to the ___ |
| Vi una película | VEE OO-nah peh-LEE-koo-lah | I watched a movie |
| Comí en | koh-MEE en | I ate at ___ |
| Estuve con mi novia | eh-STOO-veh kon mee NO-vya | I was with my girlfriend |

---

## Example Sentences
- *"Fui al parque con mi novia."* — I went to the park with my girlfriend.
- *"Vi una película en casa anoche."* — I watched a movie at home last night.
- *"Comí en ese restaurante de sushi contigo."* — I ate at that sushi restaurant with you.
- *"El fin de semana estuve con mi novia — lo mejor del finde."* — The weekend I was with my girlfriend — best part of the weekend.
