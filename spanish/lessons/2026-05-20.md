# Spanish Lesson - 2026-05-20
**Curriculum week used:** Week 12
**Topic:** Daily routines: "I'm about to..."

**Phrases Covered:**
1. **Estoy por...** (I'm about to / I'm thinking of...)
2. **Ya voy a...** (I'm going to [right now] / I'm on my way to...)
3. **Me falta poco para...** (I'm almost done [with/to]...)
4. **Acabo de...** (I just [did something]...)

**Practice Prompts:**
1. Your girlfriend asks where you are. Tell her you *just finished* work and you're *about to* leave.
2. She asks if you're ready for dinner. Tell her you *already went* to the restaurant and you're *almost done* with your walk there.

**Challenge:** LEVEL 2 - Text your girlfriend today using either "Acabo de..." or "Me falta poco para..." to update her on something you just finished or are almost done with.

**Anti-repeat check result:** Success. Today's topic (Immediate Future/Recent Past) is distinct from yesterday's (Nuanced Reactions) and Monday's (Coordination). Topic is normalized against the curriculum.
