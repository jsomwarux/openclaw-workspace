# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-25
**Week 8, Day 6 | Topic: Weekend Activities & Downtime**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿Quieres relajarte hoy?** — Do you want to relax today?
2. **Vamos a quedarnos en casa.** — Let's stay home.
3. **No tengo ganas de salir.** — I don't feel like going out.
4. **Me apetece ver una película.** — I feel like watching a movie.

## Notes
- Week 8 theme: At Home & Daily Life — capstone day
- "No tengo ganas de..." = "I don't have desire to..." = I don't feel like... — extremely natural, used constantly
- "Me apetece" = "it appeals to me" = I feel like... — common in Spain and Latin America
- "Quedarnos en casa" = "stay ourselves at home" = let's stay in — reflexive form
- Memory hook: "Ganas" = desire/urge — "no tengo ganas" = my urge tank is empty
