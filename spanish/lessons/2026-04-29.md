# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-29
**Week 9, Day 3 | Topic: Hotel & Accommodation**

## Key Phrases
1. **Tenemos una reserva.** — We have a reservation.
2. **¿Hay WiFi en la habitación?** — Is there WiFi in the room?
3. **¿A qué hora es el desayuno?** — What time is breakfast?
4. **La llave, por favor.** — The key, please.

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around
- "Tenemos una reserva" = "we have a reservation" — essential at check-in
- "¿Hay...?" = "is there...?" — universal question format for amenities
- "Desayuno" = breakfast — cognate with "desayunar" (to breakfast)
- Memory hook: "Llave" = key — sounds like "yave" with a soft y, think "unlock with love"
