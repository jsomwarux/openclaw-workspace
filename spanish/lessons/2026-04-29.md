# Spanish Lesson — 2026-04-29
**Week 9, Day 2 | Topic: At the Airport & Travel Logistics**

## Key Phrases
1. **¿A qué hora sale el vuelo?** — What time does the flight leave?
2. **Nuestro vuelo está retrasado.** — Our flight is delayed.
3. **¿Dónde está la puerta de embarque?** — Where is the boarding gate?
4. **Necesito mi pasaporte.** — I need my passport.

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around
- "¿A qué hora sale?" = "at what hour does it leave?" — standard question format for schedules
- "Está retrasado" = "is delayed/behind" — same word for bus, train, flight, meeting
- "Puerta de embarque" = "door of boarding" = boarding gate
- Memory hook: "Embarque" = embark — same root as English, easy to remember
