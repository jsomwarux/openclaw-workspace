# Lesson — 2026-03-20
**Week 3, Day 16 | Topic: Filler Words & Natural Connectors**

## Phrases Covered
| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| Pues | PWES | Well… / So… |
| Oye | OH-yeh | Hey / Listen |
| O sea | oh SEH-ah | I mean / Like / Basically |
| Es que | es keh | It's just that… / The thing is… |

## Example Sentences
- *Pues, no sé qué quiero comer.* — Well, I don't know what I want to eat.
- *Oye, ¿quieres salir esta noche?* — Hey, you wanna go out tonight?
- *Fue bien, o sea, no fue perfecto, pero bien.* — It was good, I mean, it wasn't perfect, but good.
- *Es que estaba muy cansado y no pude salir.* — It's just that I was really tired and couldn't go out.
