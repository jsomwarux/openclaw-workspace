# Spanish Lesson — 2026-05-01
**Week 9, Day 5 | Topic: Emergencies & Problems**

## Key Phrases
1. **Necesito ayuda.** — I need help.
2. **Perdí mi...** — I lost my...
3. **No me siento bien.** — I don't feel well.
4. **¿Dónde está el baño?** — Where is the bathroom?

## Notes
- Week 9 theme: Travel & Getting Around
- "Necesito ayuda" = "I need help" — direct, urgent, universally understood
- "Perdí" = I lost (past tense of perder) — followed by any object: pasaporte, teléfono, llave
- "No me siento bien" = "I don't feel well" — for illness, discomfort, anything health-related
- "¿Dónde está el baño?" = "where is the bathroom?" — the most important survival phrase, honestly
- Memory hook: "Perdí" = "I lost" — think "perdition" = lost soul. Same root.
