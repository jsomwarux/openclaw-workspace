# Spanish Lesson: 2026-05-07
**Topic:** Agreeing & Disagreeing (Week 9, Day 53)

### Phrases Covered:
1. **¿Estás de acuerdo?** (Do you agree?)
2. **Estoy de acuerdo** (I agree)
3. **No estoy de acuerdo** (I don't agree)
4. **Tienes razón** (You're right)

### Practice Prompts:

- Reacting to a movie opinion.
