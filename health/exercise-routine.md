# Exercise Routine — JT Somwaru
*Built around: gunstock deformity compensation, sympathetic nervous system bracing, voice/jaw/sinus upstream prevention*

## Core Principles
- **Unilateral everything** — bilateral lifts let the dominant side compensate
- **Posterior chain emphasis** — flips anterior dominance from sympathetic bracing
- **No jaw-clenching loads** — if your jaw braces on a rep, the weight is too heavy
- **Breathing is a rep** — full exhale before every set

## Phase Progression

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1–6):** Pattern quality only. All weights at 6/10 effort max.

**Phase 2 (Weeks 7–12):** Add load — dumbbell bench, heavier SL-RDL, goblet squat, trap bar deadlift (neutral grip accommodates carrying angle).

**Phase 3 (Month 4+):** Reassess shoulder symmetry. If improved → selective barbell introduction.

---

## Voice / Sinus / Eustachian Prevention

Upstream work: **chin tucks + face pulls + dead hangs** — these three reduce load on sub-occipitals, SCM, and scalenes that compress the upper cervical chain and affect TMJ tension and Eustachian drainage.

**In-session rule:** Jaw clenches on a rep → stop, reset, exhale, re-engage. Not optional.
