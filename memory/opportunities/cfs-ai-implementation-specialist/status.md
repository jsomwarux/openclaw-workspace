# CFS Recruiter — AI Implementation Specialist Opportunity

Last updated: 2026-05-04

## Source
Technology recruiter Dan at CFS reached out on LinkedIn.

## Role / Client Context
- Client hiring for AI Implementation Specialist in NYC.
- Client is a family-owned construction company doing luxury properties in NYC.
- They are building an AI implementation/transformation function.
- Focus: leading AI/automation initiatives, partnering with business teams, integrating AI into existing systems, measurable impact.
- Recruiter said they likely prefer full-time but would not rule consulting out.
- Recruiter believes a meeting where JT lays out vision and approach would be beneficial because client “really has no idea what they need.”

## JT Positioning
- JT responded that he is actively working with clients doing this work and is open to learning more, but needs to confirm openness to freelance/consulting vs full-time.
- Best framing: consulting diagnostic / fractional AI implementation lead, not generic candidate.
- Strongest proof: Aya construction dashboard + StreetEasy scraper; Altmark local-first operations automation; business systems analyst background.

## Meeting Goal
Convert recruiter from screening JT for a job into sponsoring JT as the person who can help the client define the function.

## Recommended Offer Shape
- Paid AI implementation diagnostic / roadmap for construction operations.
- 2-week assessment: workflows, systems, bottlenecks, priority use cases, ROI map, implementation sequence.
- Then optional implementation retainer/project.

## Watchouts
- Do not accept vague full-time framing without comp/scope clarity.
- Do not pitch “AI transformation” broadly; pitch controlled workflow implementation.
- Ask whether client can engage a consultant/fractional lead if they are not ready to define a full-time role.
