## Watch Ideas

### MAKEUPLOOKUP - WATCH, 7.1
**Value proposition:** Helps makeup buyers avoid wrong-shade and duplicate-product purchases in under one minute from a product name, shade, or shelf photo.

**Why it is promising:** The input-output loop is clean: upload or type a product, receive dupes, undertone fit notes, ingredient flags, and affiliate product links. It reuses JT's Glow Index logic, safe beauty-claim framing, and vision/OCR capability without becoming medical advice. The emotional trigger is buyer protection plus taste identity: "will this actually look right on me?"

**What blocks BUILD:** Beauty content is noisy, affiliate SEO is competitive, and dupe/shade tools may already own the obvious keywords. Without fresh saturation checks, the correct call is to prototype the data model before creating a public app task.

**MVP sandbox:** Build a private 25-product lookup table from already-known beauty signals. Test three input modes: product name, shade name, and shelf/photo OCR. Continue only if the output can beat a generic search result with specific dupe/fit/risk reasoning.

**Scorecard:** longevity 7.5, autonomy 7.0, build 7.0, marketing 7.4, revenue 6.8, uniqueness 6.5, competition resistance 5.8, agent-purchasable fit 6.8. Overall 7.1.

### DYNASTYEDGE CARDS - WATCH, 6.9
**Value proposition:** Helps dynasty fantasy players find a buy/sell/hold edge before league mates react, using market disagreement, ADP shifts, injury context, and role uncertainty.

**Why it is promising:** JT already has Sports GM and DynastyJig signal infrastructure. The product can start as structured weekly cards, not a generic rankings site. The emotional trigger is status plus early information: being the manager who spots a value gap first.

**What blocks BUILD:** The passive-income bar is autonomy. If JT has to manually write weekly takes, this is content work. It becomes a BUILD only if the card generator can pull rankings/news deltas, produce concise rationale, and publish/free-preview with minimal review.

**MVP sandbox:** Generate 10 private edge cards from current Sports GM data and score them for specificity, disagreement, and actionability. If at least 7 cards have a named player, market delta, action, and evidence trail, promote to a tiny paid report experiment.

**Scorecard:** longevity 7.2, autonomy 6.1, build 7.4, marketing 7.0, revenue 6.6, uniqueness 6.4, competition resistance 5.8, agent-purchasable fit 6.2. Overall 6.9.

### RECIPECLEAN - WATCH, 6.4
**Value proposition:** Helps home cooks turn messy saved recipe links into clean recipe cards, grocery lists, pantry tags, and meal boards before dinner planning.

**Why it is promising:** It is a narrow transformation utility with low technical complexity. Saved TikTok/Instagram/YouTube/blog recipes are chaotic, and the output can be structured JSON/markdown/cards.

**What blocks BUILD:** Recipe organization and meal planning are crowded, and social-platform ingestion may be brittle. The wedge has to be "clean my saved links" rather than a broad recipe app.

**MVP sandbox:** One-page importer for pasted recipe URLs/text only. Do not touch social auth. Continue only if users would pay $5 to clean a backlog of 30-50 recipes.

**Scorecard:** longevity 7.0, autonomy 7.2, build 7.6, marketing 5.8, revenue 5.6, uniqueness 5.5, competition resistance 4.9, agent-purchasable fit 6.5. Overall 6.4.
