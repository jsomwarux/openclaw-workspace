## Raw Ideas

### 1. ChargeTrip Fit [M6-ExplodingTopics] [M5-DataFlywheel] [M12-SocialCommerce]
**Concept:** A portable EV charger and adapter compatibility engine for renters, new EV owners, and road-trip planners. User enters vehicle, model year, parking setup, outlet type, weather exposure, trip pattern, cable-length needs, and budget; the tool returns ranked chargers/adapters plus "will this work where I actually park?" caveats.

**Signal:** `portable ev charger` is in the fresh 2026-06-06 Exploding Topics file with 3.65x growth and 22,200 monthly searches. Fresh search found NeoCharge NEMA 14-30/14-50 compatibility content, a 2026 ETL-certified portable EV charger list, and MotorTrend adapter-compatibility discussion from the week of this rerun.

**Why now:** EV owners are moving through a confusing standard transition, and renters/apartment owners often cannot install a hardwired charger. The purchase anxiety is specific: wrong outlet, wrong adapter, wrong amperage, wrong weather rating, or a cable that does not reach.

**Wedge:** Not "best portable EV charger." The wedge is a fit score for the exact car/outlet/parking/trip situation, with safety disclaimers and electrician language baked in.

**Monetization:** Affiliate links for chargers/adapters/smart splitters; $7 one-time fit report; optional $19/year recall/spec-change watchlist.

**Build shape:** Static questionnaire, normalized SKU/spec table, rules engine for plug/amperage/weather/cable warnings, comparison pages for pSEO.

**Risk:** Accuracy and safety burden. Must avoid pretending to replace an electrician and must keep recommendations conservative.

### 2. DogEarProof [M6-ExplodingTopics] [M12-SocialCommerce] [M5-TrustLayer]
**Concept:** A dog ear cleaner finder that asks breed, ear shape, swimming/grooming frequency, wax/odor symptoms, sensitivity, and vet status, then recommends maintenance products and "go to vet" thresholds.

**Signal:** `dog ear cleaner` appears in the fresh 2026-06-06 Exploding Topics file with 49,500 monthly searches. Fresh search returned AKC and PetPlace guidance from the week of this run, showing a steady consumer education need and repeated safety caveats.

**Why now:** Pet owners want a product answer but the correct guidance often depends on whether this is routine cleaning or a possible infection. That creates room for a conservative decision assistant.

**Wedge:** Routine-maintenance picker plus warning-sign triage, not diagnosis.

**Monetization:** Affiliate links for cleaners, wipes, drying products, grooming tools; breed-specific pSEO pages.

**Build shape:** Breed/ear-profile questionnaire, product database, safety copy, "ask your vet" thresholds.

**Risk:** Medical-adjacent support burden and crowded pet affiliate SEO.

### 3. BuilderGel Guard [M6-ExplodingTopics] [M9-Vision] [M12-SocialCommerce]
**Concept:** A home builder-gel kit checker for DIY nail users. User enters product ingredients, lamp type/wattage, cure time, skin-contact habits, and allergy history; output flags HEMA/acrylate risk, lamp mismatch, removal risk, and safer alternatives.

**Signal:** `builder gel` appears in the fresh Exploding Topics beauty file with 165,000 monthly searches. Fresh search found current HEMA-free builder gel content and TikTok allergy-alternative discussion.

**Why now:** At-home gel nails create a strong fear trigger: allergic reactions, poor curing, skin contact, and product/lamp mismatch. The buyer wants to keep the look without guessing.

**Wedge:** A safety-first product/lamp compatibility checker for DIY users, not another nail-inspo site.

**Monetization:** Affiliate links for HEMA-free products, lamps, removers, press-ons, and starter kits; one-time $5 kit review.

**Build shape:** Ingredient/lamp database, photo upload for label parsing as later feature, pSEO by product category.

**Risk:** Must avoid medical advice and may need careful allergy language. Beauty claim risk overlaps with existing `ClaimRisk Cards`.

### 4. MicroAirFit [M6-ExplodingTopics] [M7-GTrends] [M12-SocialCommerce]
**Concept:** A microwave-air-fryer combo selector for small kitchens, dorms, apartments, and families replacing multiple countertop appliances. User enters counter dimensions, household size, common foods, cleanup tolerance, wattage limits, and budget; output ranks appliances and explains tradeoffs.

**Signal:** `microwave air fryer` appears in the fresh Exploding Topics file with 1.97x growth and 22,200 monthly searches. Fresh search found 2026 comparison/review content from Foodsguy, Business Insider, CHOICE, and other review sources.

**Why now:** Buyers are confused by combo claims. The job is not "best air fryer"; it is "will this replace my microwave and air fryer without being terrible at one of them?"

**Wedge:** Fit and use-case scoring for constrained kitchens, not generic appliance reviews.

**Monetization:** Affiliate links for appliances and accessories; pSEO pages by kitchen constraint and product pair.

**Risk:** Review sites already compete heavily. Needs a more useful calculator than a listicle.

### 6. DocsPulse Lite [M1-ComplaintMining] [M14-TrustMRR] [M10-AgentNative]
**Concept:** A lightweight analytics layer for Notion/Slite/Confluence-style team docs that reports stale pages, ignored pages, frequently searched topics, and "ask AI but no answer found" gaps.

**Signal:** `weekly-trends.md` shows ongoing Notion/Airtable/Coda alternative demand, while DEGRADED TrustMRR lists Notionlytics at reported $39,769 MRR and multiple analytics/data/attribution comps. **Why now:** Teams are not just asking for a different wiki; they need to know whether the wiki is useful and current. AI knowledge tools make the stale-doc problem more expensive because bad docs now ground agent answers.

**Monetization:** $19-49/month for small teams; agency white-label; export-based audit for teams that do not want integrations.

**Build shape:** Start with Notion export or public docs import, stale-page scoring, top gaps, weekly email report.

**Risk:** Saturated productivity tooling and integration friction. Better as consulting-adjacent proof than pure passive income.
