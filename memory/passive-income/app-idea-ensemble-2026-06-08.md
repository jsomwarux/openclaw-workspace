## Build 1 — ChargeTrip Fit

### Buyer Promise
"Find the portable EV charger setup that actually fits your car, outlet, parking setup, and trip use case."

### MVP Scope
- One questionnaire: vehicle/plug standard, outlet type, renter/homeowner, parking distance, weather exposure, trip use, budget, and safety preference.
- One auditable rules table: NEMA 5-15, 14-30, 14-50, 6-50, J1772, NACS adapter, amperage, cable length, outdoor rating, and "call an electrician" triggers.
- 25-40 starter SKUs: portable Level 1/2 chargers, adapters, smart splitters, cable organizers, and safety accessories.
- 20 pSEO pages only after the questionnaire works.

### Monetization
- Primary: affiliate links.
- Secondary: $7 downloadable fit report after the free tool proves useful.
- Later: $19/year recall/spec-change watchlist only after traffic exists.

### Self-Running Ops Plan
- Weekly SKU refresh: price, availability, plug, amperage, certification, outdoor rating, cable length.
- Monthly rules audit: new NACS/J1772 adapter guidance, outlet constraints, safety caveats.
- Escalation to JT only when a product spec changes materially, safety language becomes uncertain, or affiliate links fail.

### Maintenance Burden
Medium-low if the rules table is conservative. High if it tries to answer electrical-code edge cases. Keep the product as compatibility education, not installation advice.

### First Action
Complete `memory/passive-income/charge-trip-fit-spec.md` with the rules table, questionnaire fields, starter SKU schema, safety boundaries, and go/no-go gate before any app scaffold.
