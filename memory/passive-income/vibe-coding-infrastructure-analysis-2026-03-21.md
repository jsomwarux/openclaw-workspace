# Vibe Coding Infrastructure Opportunities
_Analyzed: 2026-03-21_

## Core Insight
Vibe coders can ship apps in a weekend. The gap is **operating the app** after launch:
- Getting users (distribution)
- Understanding users (feedback synthesis)
- Keeping users (retention intelligence)
- Outpacing competitors (competitive monitoring)
- Making the app better (feature prioritization)

PostBridge solves one slice of distribution. The "operating the app" layer is wide open.

---

## Ranked Opportunities

### 1. Vibe Marketing Engine ⭐ BUILD FIRST
**What it is:** Product-aware content engine — understands your app, generates NB2 scene photos, writes platform-native copy, schedules across TikTok/X/LinkedIn/Reddit, tracks performance.
**Gap:** Buffer/Later schedule content. Agencies cost $3-5K/month. Nothing in between that understands your product and auto-generates native content.
**Pricing:** $49-99/month
**Target:** Anyone who shipped via Lovable, Bolt, or Cursor with no time for marketing
**JT advantage:** Already built 80% for Nash Satoshi and Vista — just needs a connect-your-product onboarding flow
**Why first:** Built, differentiated, low churn once content is flowing, doubles as consulting proof point

### 2. Ensemble Scoring API
**What it is:** Submit any items + scoring criteria → ranked scores with 4-model consensus, confidence level, and divergence notes
**Gap:** Nobody productizing multi-model deliberation as an API
**Use cases:** Product/feature ranking, app idea validation, "build X or Y" decisions, content idea scoring
**Pricing:** $0.05-0.10/analysis OR $29-99/month volume plan
**JT advantage:** Glow Index and Nash Satoshi pipelines already prove the architecture. Stage 1 research + Stage 2/3 deliberation + Stage 4 consensus is battle-tested.
**B2B angle:** Consulting upsell — sell decision-making infrastructure to clients

### 3. Review & Feedback Intelligence
**What it is:** Connect App Store + Google Play + Reddit + support tickets → weekly synthesis: "here's what users actually want, ranked by signal strength"
**Differentiation:** Ensemble pipeline on reviews — 4 models deliberating finds real signal vs noise + "what to build next" recommendation
**Pricing:** $29-79/month
**Why it works:** Replaces a junior PM. Indie builders pay this without hesitation.

### 4. Competitive Intelligence for Indie Builders
**What it is:** Monitor 5 competitors — price changes, new features (changelog scraping), review trends, social mentions, App Store ranking shifts. Weekly digest + real-time alerts.
**Gap:** Enterprise tools (Klue, Crayon) cost $1K+/month. Nothing for vibe coders at $19-49/month.
**Pricing:** $19-49/month
