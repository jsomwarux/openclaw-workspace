TikTok keeps turning small ideas into global hits, and these five apps each
found a simple video format people actually want to watch.
Wakio → The Alarm That Makes You Move

Wakio
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/nq1DVC1ylK1TLCIHoNC1C7y8iyQa9XAA%2FtuNWjSecTy%2Ft4jlbkRZcvK4TyE1%0AxBtxKh5k0gXP9MRhPL43F0AD72qVOoTxzFs0cVGFx9EKbxNIsRCwfRRquUY0%0AdIjfHtb3ca9omZ8orX7ZlxGYp%2FDA%2F%2BzFx1i5Sbt4%2BHsa29Cndn51WzDLsREU%0AlH%2B9pa4AOmcq4pfCL2RMYFrzzfovwh1mmg%3D%3D%0A/ee0dcbc915012f23>
is a mission-based alarm app: to turn it off, you complete a task like
taking a photo of the sky or scanning a barcode.

One viral video by @vidsbyelisaa
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/6YwLkQazpLrHbd1WzWFSZrGqJaPyJFaImusc%2BNDEJCqZR5I5fXl9TnyaIak6%0AFkps0WKu3WTnR0R0kTUuI1UbvGvhlW2tAtyUgxpkaPD4I7SejEmy%2Fav8oOof%0AA%2F%2FsT4OWIJloRa6%2BlV4utjve4R3HzB0DXg8Dwy55%2B%2BmbyF4kMl9eSZ3yp4U2%0AtcCM6Ww3pwJHcFlGte%2B%2BqbhN3W0lGhYpPw%3D%3D%0A/4bb290876e67880e>
showed exactly that: an alarm going off, someone dragging themselves to the
window, taking a photo of the sky, and finally turning it off.

That format spread across 20 accounts, was translated into German, and
pushed Wakio into the Top 100 in multiple countries.
Push Up Arena → Workouts Into Boss Fights

Push Up Arena
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/c%2BSxQNNo2ZnLLjZGrTALrbfp2Wq7%2B0YOX4XztX9unpdCxbdw%2BnIxUFiUYuXj%0AlpH%2FwV%2F69Vij%2B9zxllMmG3Z8Sn2QXM5sfsSth4MUlqa0CJepC%2B6RWpBxPpHu%0AfBI3%2F7rdsjsko1Qs%2FrFow8huy5FOlS7BmO0WQCbHwLNtB%2FJ2LKSaGCJY5B5X%0AFm67bPuDb0r79m8qHwsAzrO2l%2Bbjp8mqaw%3D%3D%0A/8a38d2501c4a74bc>
turns push-ups into an AR boss battle.

One video showed a guy doing push-ups while a skeleton fought a goblin on
screen, reaching 11M views
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/GW9YqkNja3mFWpXQt542EsCAp4MAKYkxqe3F7ojj%2BAaOWbkqGjI8tV%2Ff%2FSrG%0ApI02Jv8mqPPHWga6kJrPxh8s0mYxv4F0XSxCEYW%2FW3K6ryTMDYkGtGbfREZh%0A3Wl0cw9pjA8dLHIC6eEvlBVGX9TO%2Fbp68RBvsehNjKDeNYSMAQrDDjVLR7Wl%0AC005P1GRfpwH%2FqwqMU5OqMWeEd%2B1WGmTOw%3D%3D%0A/654fd75b7f56bb8b>
across TikTok and Instagram.

You immediately understand what’s happening, which makes it easy to stop
scrolling and keep watching.
Pose Genius → The Pose Coach For Pictures

Pose Genius
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/%2BoGJleQz1nHBWNdMeZKaOlYgARTMR598XM%2BbTloghnA3P2ZH7TZixoG2hqGg%0AZuwMJHfSbp47xXWnq3BbpCH6OpjRU2E6wYnht4a%2B01UZgkPuICIB1Rz%2BChce%0A%2BvMkAtHMpA3fC9ji6lteb4BAnnewf8PL2b%2B3wx8wlLb59Nypo%2BDoBDGOUGDf%0A0frLNLwD3beTB5mnhoq31TiIQocxH5Cq5Q%3D%3D%0A/d5d9164b87b97b7a>
is an AI pose-coaching app that scans your surroundings and suggests poses
while you take photos.

Its videos tap into something almost everyone relates to: not knowing how
to pose for pictures.

Most creators follow the same formula: they admit they’re awkward in
photos, show a few stiff poses, then open the app to fix it.

Two creator accounts have already generated 7.7M views
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/eVJTBA%2BiRiC7iuQehN3MXEa2QhMR9C%2FKP9Yeildys2RBdy8p%2B%2F15P9CNQrzU%0AavGV577hl2cSk5FaVzEY%2BAyQF3dHCYB4kBIGK3F7SbMbtnq0ZwNH0%2BBWDYMA%0AbQp%2FFOmW7pS21G3hquhW9Sbh%2FZE3q%2B3h53F2HLrTEwG%2FMCD0BaE1WBjT5zDy%0AtAI4sgVYV9neBFwD%2FUHMj2DVrN2QTiy7JA%3D%3D%0A/2bdf217f251bbbd4>,
with another 17 accounts still posting the same format.

Roost
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/Cl9yKKO6DMz30mqW7GWSwj2QP4g1rEh6iltc9ErGaalG2IglFql%2BtLYu89Wx%0A8ORsJMabDucVbB7ql4Q5larnuYKxuNpgfGa%2FeQHbVzQvFfmCCeHAHUmX6c27%0As5wZFdXXHhpzoubiGdvnVm%2BgLyWVSCps70K4hzmNaXtHsZriBcKMMT6%2BJ0QM%0AucfCsxv4QDSYSlHywXBgsl9PGJkg0dBrCg%3D%3D%0A/2bcb7a6c6f417bd2>

instantly.

Founder Logan Mendelsohn
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/4mHStVbiLcWMvBJCjbhR614WZmnqoccE4bY9x8j3uxUEiQ%2Fxs%2FUPca8DyV9B%0ASfYlT5br2CdtRaKK7AEdbpc4jRWw6CgoZ6gXoacXxuq0di7NBjBEUuxMnrtD%0ANQ%2BHqp%2BlIgatLPV%2BAKfB%2FS8aC9tCJ%2BigKA7xrlBHLgvWCfgJYWOvERmTj08S%0AOLrnPCTzGi1c%2Bqy3qQmpToGvlG%2BEb6nGdA%3D%3D%0A/230bfd438dee5adb>

Just two videos have generated 2.2M views
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/br9121lTu2ONUViMDiYZpgT97hdcKqaPzAhLCLb42HemxjMqHmhKMcILUizE%0A3THRyJVx8gIjGgWd8Bwxfj2dt665%2Bko8g2kuzKM1sa2TkCUCUJJcC4y7pSb0%0Ae0JaMzH2dt6Q16YE6U9xOGHiomNWnUMidkfx5s8TFUOzvoEU0imh4NvDj1gf%0AWVGE6Yug4hKIPUaP%2FGGQu070haRqOwUcEA%3D%3D%0A/67fdb6cad6636c09>,
proving that sometimes a simple founder demo is all you need.
FaceKit → The 3D Facial Scanning App

FaceKit
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/L4TLTBSA6sj5DNO7ibtXgM5dj9rMcZEezk7VcVj3imOO8trUZ20%2B5MagQoJO%0AKnUzuP3pTMZBPi7cpNhZ3CMTrEN4ik4T%2BdpCGM3n4MV%2FHDctLakSNW%2BV0x8R%0AYSkX8itRHJVyOJZ4vvn3eU1jCXbXPYwThEWbAPe59QXFgl09%2BJNhNknt6OJj%0AiVcYM9O0cPy2OZ%2BH37K5rofNt0Ri3MNNVw%3D%3D%0A/b34ccdc3f9f91fac>

one proven format: glow-up slideshows.

The videos move in sync with the music, making them feel more like TikTok
edits than app demos.

Its two strongest videos reached 959K
<https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/v1/c/yrI9Xm8OpwptrY3I5gXHFZBCAbAMN6NPjVXdKWkStsygu%2F66DkctWOIm4NHp%0AzGIfZq5tI3eVA74KW7uTT%2BMMiJkgzAwsICt6YHwoI7jDjrgHskaShiU0XeRf%0A1dtt2aBy2VstJ7vJKPZ4X6TigF7QXnN48AZLrIvs%2FrKKz%2B%2FGvYkK3OYrSuOH%0ArWEWUy5Mhbb8t1%2F4u%2FiHAJxSRVOTuCSMTg%3D%3D%0A/83c2a1fe950f0890>and
1.6M views.
What These Apps Have In Common

None of these apps relied on dozens of different content ideas.

Instead, they each found one format that people enjoyed watching, then kept
repeating it across creators, accounts, and countries.

Once a format starts working, consistency often beats constantly chasing
the next idea.

TikTok is a long game, so nothing is ever guaranteed. We’ll keep you
updated.
