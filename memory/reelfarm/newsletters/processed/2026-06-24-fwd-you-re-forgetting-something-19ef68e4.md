1. We Have Other Options

This trend started in restaurant setups, but it can be recreated for other
verticals.

A girl says her boyfriend will not let her get dessert, then the waiter
answers:

“*Don’t worry, we have other options.*”

The camera cuts to the “other options,” a.k.a other male staff members.

*How to use:* show the better version. Dating apps can show better matches.
Food apps can show better orders. Travel apps can show better trips.

2. You Think I’m Pretty

This trend is about selective hearing.

Someone says:

“*I think you’re pretty addicted to coffee.*”

The creator only hears:

“*You think I’m pretty?*”

*How to use:* tap into an audience obsession. Coffee, shopping, skincare,
fitness, books, studying, or anything people get called out for liking too
much.

*How to use:* go for small triggers your audience overthinks. Flight
delays, plans changing, bad texts, study distractions, or missed routines.

The summer packing trend is back.

Creators show what they packed, then add:

“*All packed, but I’m forgetting something…*”

Then they reveal the person, pet, product, habit, or app they wish they
could bring.

*How to use:* connect the reveal to summer plans. Travel, photo, pet,
productivity, and lifestyle apps can all dive into it.
