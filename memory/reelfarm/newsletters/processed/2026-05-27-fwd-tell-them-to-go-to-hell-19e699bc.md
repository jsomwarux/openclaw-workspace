4. How ___ Sounds To Smart, Funny People

This trend is built around a simple compliment hook:

“How ___ sounds to people who are smart, funny, and pretty.”

Creators fill the blank with a song, artist, niche interest, habit, or
random obsession.

It works because the hook turns a preference into an identity flex. The
creator is not just saying they like something, they are saying the right
kind of person gets it.

*How to use:* turn your app, niche, or user behavior into the thing only
“smart, funny, pretty” people understand, then use the format to make your
audience feel in on the joke.
