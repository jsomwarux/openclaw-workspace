# App Marketing / Mobile Growth

## Current Thesis
App marketing needs measurement-backed experiments and durable discovery, not more random posting. Competitor intel should answer what is already working in adjacent apps, then map one pattern to a named experiment.

## Buyer/User Pains
- Builders ship apps with no distribution loop.
- TikTok/ReelFarm volume without metrics creates noise.
- ASO/SEO/AI-search surfaces compound slowly but matter.
- Competitor signals are hard to turn into experiments.

## Common Workflows
- AppKittie competitor scan.
- ASO screenshot/title/subtitle review.
- ReelFarm/TikTok hook tests.
- SEO/directory submission packs.
- Weekly scoreboard and experiment calendar.

## Tools / Systems Used
AppKittie, App Store Connect, TikTok/ReelFarm, Reddit, X, Notion/Drive, SEO pages, directory listings.

## Automation Opportunities
- Weekly competitor teardown report.
- Hook → experiment calendar mapping.
- Post registry → metric collector → optimization rules.
- ASO/GEO checklist automation.

## Objections / Trust Issues
- AppKittie revenue/downloads are estimates.
- No broad scans without credit control.
- Do not automate TikTok posting from Mac mini; JT laptop owns ReelFarm execution.
