# Wholesale Distribution

## Current Thesis
Wholesale distributors often have valuable catalog/order/vendor knowledge trapped in reps’ heads, spreadsheets, ERPs, and inboxes. AI value is in order/status exceptions, catalog lookup, ETA replies, and institutional knowledge capture.

## Buyer/User Pains
- Customers ask for stock/ETA/order status and reps manually chase answers.
- Catalog knowledge lives with senior reps or owners.
- Substitute/product-fit knowledge is hard to access after hours.
- Inventory/reorder signals are scattered.
- Quotes and follow-ups age silently.

## Common Workflows
- Order status reply drafting.
- Stock/ETA/vendor lookup.
- Reorder and supplier notification.
- Catalog Q&A or substitute recommendation.
- Quote follow-up queues.

## Tools / Systems Used
ERP, inventory systems, spreadsheets, email, supplier portals, CRMs, product catalogs/PDFs.

## Automation Opportunities
- Order status exception desk.
- Catalog knowledge assistant with source citations.
- Reorder threshold alerts and supplier drafts.
- Customer reply drafts requiring rep approval.

## Objections / Trust Issues
- Wrong inventory info damages trust.
- Product substitutions need source/confidence and human approval.
- ERP integration can be messy.
