## Sunday | Behind the Build | [multi-trade-dispatch-routing-before-urgency]

Multi-trade dispatch isn't a coordination problem.

It's a routing problem.

Every job potentially needs two separate trade crews, two parts catalogs, two scheduling queues. The emergency and the scheduled arrive at the same time, and the person on the phone has to separate them by trade type before they can separate them by urgency.

The mistake in building this workflow is starting with urgency. You start with trade. Emergency HVAC and emergency plumbing are different jobs that need different dispatch logic.

Build the routing layer first. Urgency classification runs after.
