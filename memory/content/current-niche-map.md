# Current Content Niche Map
> Source of truth for JT's content generation, swipe-file collection, and LinkedIn/X reference selection.
> Load this before choosing content niches. Current efforts are inputs, not the strategy.

## Priority Order

### Tier 1 - Consulting Revenue + Proof
These are the strongest niches for LinkedIn credibility, referral surface, and paid-client conversion.

1. **SMB AI Implementation**
   - Includes: AI Consulting, AI Implementation, AI Ops, workflow automation, n8n implementation, practical agents, dashboards, operations automation.
   - Use when: the post teaches the implementation gap, workflow-first delivery, services-as-software, or proof from real client work.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn, then X.

2. **Property Management Operations**
   - Includes: rent delinquency, insurance expirations, lease renewals, maintenance exceptions, owner/resident/vendor follow-up, AppFolio/Buildium/Rent Manager workflow layers.
   - Use when: Altmark, Aya, real estate ops, family office operations, or PM AI adoption signals are relevant.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

3. **NYC SMB Operations**
   - Includes: owner-led SMBs, ops-heavy local businesses, messy handoffs, approvals, inbox/spreadsheet/portal work, bilingual or NYC-specific operating realities.
   - Use when: the content should speak to buyers without over-narrowing by vertical.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

4. **Wholesale Distribution Operations**
   - Includes: inventory exceptions, PO/vendor follow-up, catalog cleanup, order intake, dispatch/ETA handoffs, document-to-system workflows.
   - Use when: distribution/warehouse/vendor coordination signals appear or when a buyer-facing example needs a non-real-estate lane.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

5. **Construction + Skilled Trades Operations**
   - Includes: field-to-office handoffs, estimates, change-order evidence, job status, crew communication, document extraction, adoption/training.
   - Use when: construction/trades signals appear or a post needs a field-ops buyer angle.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

6. **Insurance / Agentforce Operations**
   - Includes: Salesforce/Agentforce activation, claims intake, policy servicing, renewal prep, audit trails, broker servicing, Novidea/Salesforce-adjacent insurance workflows.
   - Use when: the prospect/signal is stack-qualified or the post is about governance and implementation in regulated workflows.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

### Tier 2 - Authority + Career Leverage
These support consulting and selective $150K+ AI implementation roles.

7. **AI Operating Systems / Agent Orchestration**
   - Includes: OpenClaw, specialized agents, cron systems, MCP/connectors, evals, human approval loops, agent governance, rollback, reliability.
   - Use when: the post proves JT can design operating systems around AI without positioning him as a pure developer.
   - Primary platform: X for builder audience, LinkedIn when tied to business adoption.

8. **AI Enablement / Solutions Architecture Career**
   - Includes: AI Solutions Architect, AI Implementation Lead, adoption, workflow mapping, governance, connector strategy, ROI, implementation leadership.
   - Use when: the post should attract hiring managers or translate role-market intel into public authority.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn.

9. **Productized Services / Solo Operator Systems**
   - Includes: services-as-software, reusable delivery IP, client OS, proof capture, retention systems, operator-builder leverage.
   - Use when: the post teaches how JT packages consulting into repeatable systems.
   - Primary platform: LinkedIn and X.

### Tier 3 - Owned Product + Passive Income
These matter, but they should not outrank consulting/proof unless the task is explicitly app marketing.

10. **App Growth / App Marketing OS**
    - Includes: durable discovery, ASO, pSEO, ReelFarm/TikTok experiments, measurement, launch mechanics, borrowed audiences.
    - Use when: the work is explicitly about Vista, Nash, Glow, app experiments, or passive-income app distribution.

11. **Vista / Movie Taste Apps**
    - Includes: movie rating, 1-100 scale, taste identity, Letterboxd alternatives, watch-history signal.
    - Use only for Vista-specific app content or product-builder proof.

12. **Nash Satoshi / Crypto Ranking**
    - Includes: crypto rankings, model disagreement, ranking receipts, methodology, x402-adjacent operator-builder signals.
    - Use for Nash content, crypto content, or proof of multi-model ranking systems.

13. **Glow Index / Skincare Ranking**
    - Includes: skincare product ranking, evidence-first product evaluation, ingredient skepticism, buyer protection.
    - Use only with claim-safety guardrails; no medical/diagnosis/treatment claims.

### Tier 4 - Separate Brand / Optional Lanes
These are relevant only when the request or channel explicitly asks for them.

14. **x402 / Agentic Payments**
    - Use for @jt__crypto, Nash-adjacent infrastructure posts, or operator-builder commentary. Not a generic SMB consulting offer.

15. **Dynasty Fantasy / Sports GM**
    - Use for @dynastyjig/Sports GM content only. Do not cross-pollinate into LinkedIn consulting patterns.

16. **Guyana Local Content Operations**
    - Use for Guyana supplier/local-content strategy and review-only outreach packets. Not a default LinkedIn niche until the page/offer is rewritten and approved.

## Explicit Non-Defaults
- **Claude Code** is a tool/proof ingredient, not a current primary niche.
- **Vista, Glow Index, Nash Satoshi, and App Marketing** are active product lanes, not the default content universe.
- **Generic AI Agents** is too broad unless tied to SMB operations, agent orchestration, governance, or a real build.
- **Personal Brand** is a container, not a niche. Always map it to a real lane above.

## Selection Rules
1. If content is for LinkedIn and no niche is specified, choose from Tier 1 or Tier 2 first.
2. If content is for X and no niche is specified, choose from Tier 1, Tier 2, Nash/x402, or builder proof based on the strongest current evidence.
3. If the draft uses a Tier 3 product niche, it must label the business purpose: product growth, app proof, passive-income test, or builder credibility.
4. If same-platform same-niche swipe examples are thin, create or update a Mission Control task to collect that corpus instead of substituting unrelated product/tool niches.
5. Cross-niche references are allowed only as `ADJACENT_REFERENCE_ONLY` and must include a JT translation explaining why the mechanic applies.
