# Weekly Content Seeds

> Drop 2-4 raw observations here by Sunday night.
> These become the raw material for the week's content — Monday's cron reads this first.
> No formatting needed. Write exactly how you'd say it in a voice memo.
>
> What qualifies as a seed:
> - Something you built or shipped this week (even small)
> - A pattern you noticed across prospects or clients
> - Something that surprised you, annoyed you, or clicked
> - A lesson from a failed attempt
> - A conversation that made you think differently
>
> What does NOT qualify:
> - Generic AI industry observations (the cron already pulls those)
> - Things you haven't actually done or seen yourself
