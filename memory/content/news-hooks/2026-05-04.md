
## [09:30] - Agent oversight layer launches
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** AI agent management is becoming its own software category.
**Source:** https://itbrief.com.au/story/microsoft-launches-agent-365-to-oversee-ai-agents
**X Thread:**
AI agent management is becoming its own software category.

A major software vendor launched an oversight layer to discover and manage local agents, including agents built with n8n and customer service tools.
---
The reason is simple.

AI agents move faster than human users, connect to outside services, and create new security paths your team may not see from a normal app inventory.
---
Your agent stack needs an inventory before it needs more agents.

Track what each agent can access, where it can send data, and which human owns the approval gate.
