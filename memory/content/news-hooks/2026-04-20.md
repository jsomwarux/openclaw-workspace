
## [09:33] - Most AI projects die between pilot and production
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** 1.7x ROI exists for firms that scale AI past pilot — most never get there
**Source:** https://masterofcode.com/blog/ai-roi
**X Thread:**
Most AI projects die between pilot and production. The ones that survive aren't running better AI. They're running better integrations.
---
A meta-review of 16 research reports found that firms moving AI from pilot to production see 1.7x ROI. Most firms never cross that gap. The blocker is almost never the AI itself.
---
Your team doesn't need another AI tool. They need someone who can connect AI to your actual workflow and get it past the pilot graveyard. That gap is where the ROI lives.
