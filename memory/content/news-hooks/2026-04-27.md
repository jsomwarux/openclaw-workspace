
## [09:30] - AI answering service ROI for plumbers
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** AI automation ROI is easiest to see where missed calls leak revenue.
**Source:** https://javalogix.ca/ai-automation-roi
**X Thread:**
AI automation ROI gets real when you tie it to missed calls.

A service business model showed a $700/month AI answering system recovering roughly $12,000 in first-month revenue.
---
The math works because service businesses miss 20% to 40% of calls.

One missed plumbing job can cost $300 to $2,000. Faster follow-up compounds the gain when your team responds inside five minutes.
---
Your first AI workflow should attach to a revenue leak, not a productivity wish.

Find where demand already exists. Then use automation to capture work your business already earned.
