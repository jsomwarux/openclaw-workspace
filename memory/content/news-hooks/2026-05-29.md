
## 09:30 - Research lab finds AI agent token costs varied up to 30x
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** Agent cost controls need meters, budgets, and receipt trails before SMB workflows get agents.
**Source:** https://digitaleconomy.stanford.edu/news/how-are-ai-agents-spending-your-tokens/
**X Thread:**
A research lab just put numbers on agent cost drift: the same coding agent task varied by up to 30x. Your AI budget needs a meter before your workflow gets an agent.
---
The paper says agents cannot predict token costs because context grows while they work. Models also underestimate spend. Your simple task can burn tokens after the agent starts wandering.
---
For operators and AI consultants: give every agent a budget, stop rule, and receipt trail. Your client should see what the system spent, what it tried, and where a human took over. (via @DigEconLab)
