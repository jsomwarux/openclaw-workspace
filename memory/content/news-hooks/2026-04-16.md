
## [09:30] - One Major Platform Just Put a Price on AI That Actually Works
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** AI vendors are starting to bet their own revenue on outcomes. That changes everything about how you evaluate them.
**Source:** https://aiagentstore.ai/ai-agent-news/2026-april
**X Thread:**
A major CRM platform just changed how it charges for AI. No more monthly fees. You pay 50 cents per conversation resolved, one dollar per qualified lead. The vendor only makes money when the AI works. That is a different conversation than what most AI vendors are having right now.

---
Companies paid monthly for AI tools that did not work. When enough buyers got burned, the pricing model started shifting. Outcome-based AI is the correction. Vendors put skin in the game or they lose the deal.

---
If you are buying AI tools right now, ask the vendor this: will you charge me only when it works? If the answer is no, you know exactly how confident they are in their own product.
