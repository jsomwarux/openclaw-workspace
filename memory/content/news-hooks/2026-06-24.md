
## 09:30 - Shared AI agent lands inside workplace chat
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** Shared chat agents make implementation boundaries more important than tool selection.
**Source:** https://itbrief.asia/story/anthropic-launches-claude-tag-for-slack-workplace-workflows
**X Thread:**
A major AI lab just put a shared agent inside workplace chat. Employees can tag it in a thread, hand it a task, and keep the work where the team already talks.
---
The research preview targets Team and Enterprise accounts. Admins get controls for access and spend, and the agent can break a thread into steps or surface org updates.
---
For your team, the implementation work starts with the thread, owner, approval rule, and budget limit. That boundary decides whether the agent helps or creates cleanup. (via @ITBriefAsia)
