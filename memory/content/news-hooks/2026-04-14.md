
## [09:30] - 62% Experiment With AI Agents. Less Than 25% Have Scaled.
**Status:** Ready to post
**Hook:** The gap between AI agent experimentation and production deployment is the consulting opportunity. Most companies are stuck in the middle.
**Source:** https://www.barchart.com/story/news/1204699/belitsoft-releases-ai-agent-development-forecast-2026-40-of-enterprise-applications-to-include-task-specific-agents-by-year-end
**X Thread:**
AI agents are moving into production. By the end of this year, 40% of business applications will include task-specific agents, up from under 5% in 2025. The question is not whether. It is who gets there first. (via @Belitsoft)

---
The gap is real. Sixty-two percent of organizations are experimenting with AI agents. Fewer than 25% have scaled to production. More than 40% of agentic AI projects will stall this year because costs are climbing and the business value is unclear without better risk controls.

---
If you are advising companies on AI adoption right now, you are not selling automation. You are selling the path from experiment to production. That is a different skill and a different conversation than most vendors are having.
