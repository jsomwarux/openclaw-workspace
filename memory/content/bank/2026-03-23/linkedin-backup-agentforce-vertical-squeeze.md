The clients asking for "AI consulting" last year are asking for something more specific now.

Three months ago, the top Agentforce listings said "Agentforce Consultant" with no domain attached. Today, the highest-compensation roles are domain-specific: retail AI architect, insurance implementation lead, supply chain SA. The pattern shows in job postings, SI partner requirements, and how RFPs are being scoped.

The generalist AI consultant tier is getting compressed from both sides. Platform vendors want specialists who already know the domain. Businesses want someone who has built for their specific problem.

The window for a generalist to claim a vertical before domain specialists arrive is 6 to 12 months, depending on how fast buying cycles move in that sector.

Platform knowledge is table stakes now. Vertical depth is the thing that holds.
