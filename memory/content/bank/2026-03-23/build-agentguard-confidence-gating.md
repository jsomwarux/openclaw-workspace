AI agents stall in enterprise before they ship. legal wants a paper trail. compliance wants to know who owns the outcome when it's wrong.

AgentGuard answers both.

above 70% confidence: the agent routes without a person.
below 70%: goes to human review before anything moves.
every decision logged — what it scored, what it did, who reviewed it.

claims come in. the agent classifies, scores, and routes. a coordinator only sees the ambiguous ones.
