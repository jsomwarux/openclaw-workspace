## Mini-thread: Market Inefficiency

1/ Dynasty market inefficiency is not “I like this player more than consensus.”

It is a gap between market price and probable future value.

2/ Market price comes from KTC, FantasyCalc, ADP, trade databases, and sentiment.

Future value comes from usage, role security, age curve, team context, liquidity, and catalysts.

3/ The edge is when those two disagree for a reason the market is misweighting.

Not every disagreement is an edge.
Some are just you being wrong.

4/ The goal is not to have takes.

The goal is to build a repeatable decision system with receipts.

## Mini-thread: Roster Construction

1/ Every dynasty team is one of five things:

Fragile contender
Productive struggle
Rebuild
Trapped middle

2/ A true contender should consolidate for points.
A fragile contender should buy insulation.
A rebuild should buy liquidity.
