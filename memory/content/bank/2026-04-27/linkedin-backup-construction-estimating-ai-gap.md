Construction firms say estimating is about speed, but the real gap is trust in the numbers.

New AI tools are moving into blueprint-to-quantity workflows because estimating pain is no longer hidden in back-office spreadsheets. It is now a front-line margin problem.

The signal is clear: construction AI is moving from project summaries into bidding workflows.
