The dispatch problem at a dual-trade shop is twice as hard as people think.

One crew handles plumbing. Another handles HVAC. Both get emergency calls.
Both have their own parts inventory. Both need status updates to the client.

Most field service software treats every job the same.
The actual routing needs three decisions simultaneously:
1. Emergency or scheduled?
2. Which trade?
3. Which crew is closest and available?

If you're solving that with a dispatcher and a whiteboard at $16M revenue — that's the gap.
