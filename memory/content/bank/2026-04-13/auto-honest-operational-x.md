The scariest automation failure is the one that looks like success.

n8n StreetEasy scraper ran for 3 months. Zero errors. Green checkmarks every time.

One day I opened the sheet. Nothing had been added in 6 weeks.

The fix was not a better workflow. It was a daily Telegram ping that shows me what the scraper actually found that day.

Automation without monitoring is an expensive illusion.
