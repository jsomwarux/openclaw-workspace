Commercial contractors running two trades have a dispatch problem single-trade shops don't.

Every incoming job needs three answers at once: emergency or scheduled? Which trade? Which crew is available?

Field service software picks one routing variable. Dual-trade operators need all three. The gap is where dispatch calls get made by hand.
