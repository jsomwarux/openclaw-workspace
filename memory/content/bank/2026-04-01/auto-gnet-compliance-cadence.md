Commercial GCs track hospital renovations and school renovations differently. They just don't do it automatically.

Hospitals have infection control sign-offs. Schools have occupancy windows.
Tenant improvements have lease milestones. Maintenance has SLA clocks.

Most job trackers treat all four the same.
The insight isn't faster tracking. It's tagged tracking.
