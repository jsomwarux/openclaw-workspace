## Signal used
Property-management AI coverage is clustering around resident-lifecycle platforms, maintenance triage, vendor coordination, repeat-issue detection, and task routing. The better consulting wedge is not generic AI automation. It is a maintenance exception desk for PM operators.

## LinkedIn draft

The maintenance inbox is where property management margins quietly disappear.

A tenant submits a request. Someone decides urgency. Someone checks the unit history. Someone texts a vendor. Someone follows up when the vendor misses the window. Someone explains the delay to the tenant.

That is not one task. It is a chain of handoffs.

The AI opportunity is an approval-boundary workflow: classify the request, flag repeat issues, suggest the vendor route, draft the tenant update, and keep a human approving anything expensive or ambiguous.

Agents handle the routing. Operators keep the judgment.

That is the kind of AI system property managers can actually trust in production.
