The best AI consulting prospects are not always tech-forward companies.

Sometimes it is a 120-year-old trade business with 40 staff, 22 trucks, and three service lines running at once.

The gap is rarely “they need a chatbot.”

The gap is the workflow layer after the first call: which truck owns the job, which estimate needs follow-up, which old service visit should surface before the customer calls again.

Old businesses do not need generic AI. They need their operating memory to stop living in the dispatcher’s head.
