The person with the most credentials in a construction firm shouldn't be routing permits.

At a multi-sector GC running hospitals, schools, tenant improvements, and emergency restoration, permit coordination lands on whoever understands each job type's compliance requirements. If no one built the classification layer, that's the principal.

G-Net Construction Corp, NYC. 65 years in business. The architecture principal manages permit review.

Hospital jobs have different inspection cadences than school jobs. Different reporting formats. Most field service software doesn't know the difference.
