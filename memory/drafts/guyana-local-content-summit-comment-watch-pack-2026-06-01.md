For offshore service operators, reliability is often a coordination problem before it is a technology problem.

Curious how local suppliers are managing that as volume grows.

### 5. AmCham / business bridge post

The useful bridge here is not only connecting local suppliers to opportunity.

That is where smaller operators can lose time even when demand is there.
