## What Vista Is (Current, Accurate)

Movie ratings + taste profiles. Live on the Apple App Store.

Key features:
- Rate movies as you watch them
- All-time highest rated film in your profile
- Recent highest rated (last 30 days) — shows what you're into right now
- Favorite performer — calculated from your ratings, not hand-picked
- No social feed. No public lists. Just your personal film record.

Positioning: Letterboxd is a social network. Vista is a personal taste tracker.
Target: iOS users who want to understand their own taste, not perform it for an audience.

## 5 — Product Hunt Listing (READY TO SUBMIT)

**Name:** Vista

**Tagline:** Movie ratings that build your taste profile

**Description (short):**
Vista is a personal movie tracker that shows you who you are as a film person — not what the crowd thinks. Rate movies, and Vista surfaces your all-time favorites, what you're into right now, and your favorite performer (calculated from your data, not hand-picked). No social feed. Just your taste.

**Description (long):**
Most movie apps are about tracking what you've watched. Vista is about understanding who you are as a film person.

Rate movies as you watch them, and Vista surfaces three things most apps miss:

**All-time highest rated** — your definitive #1 film based on your actual ratings, not what you think you should love.

**Recent highest rated (last 30 days)** — your current phase. Taste shifts. Vista shows you what you're into right now, not just what you've loved forever.

**Favorite performer** — Vista analyzes every highly-rated film in your library and identifies which performer appears most in your top films. You don't pick them. Your ratings do. The answer often surprises people.

No social feed. No public lists. No followers. Just your personal film record.

Built because I wanted it and nothing else existed that felt this personal.

Available on the App Store: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/vista-movie-taste-profiles/id6758186885
