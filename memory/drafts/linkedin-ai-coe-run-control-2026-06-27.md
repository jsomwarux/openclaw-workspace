Built a workflow portfolio view this morning around a simple question: which AI use case is allowed to move first?

A remote Principal Consultant AI role I reviewed this morning asked for readiness discovery, use-case portfolio design, governance, ROI stress testing, and adoption roadmaps.

That is the right shape.

The hard part is turning those words into an operating surface.

Which workflow is approved to move?
Which source record starts it?
Which tool is allowed to touch it?
Who approves the sensitive case?
What result counts as value?

I would rather show a client 12 rows of scored workflow candidates, approval owners, blocked actions, and estimated dollar impact than a deck saying "AI transformation."

The portfolio is the work.

The roadmap is only believable when every proposed use case already has a source, owner, control rule, and value event.
