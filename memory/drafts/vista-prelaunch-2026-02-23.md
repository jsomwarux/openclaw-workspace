## 5 — Product Hunt Listing Draft
*Submit ~24–48h before App Store goes live if possible, or day-of*

**Name:** Vista

**Tagline:** Movie ratings that build your taste profile

**Description (short):**
Vista is a cleaner alternative to Letterboxd. Rate movies, and Vista builds a taste profile around your ratings: your all-time highest rated film, what you're into right now (last 30 days), and your favorite performer — calculated from the data, not hand-picked.

**Description (long):**
Most movie apps are about tracking what you've watched. Vista is about understanding *who you are* as a film person.

Rate movies as you watch them, and Vista surfaces three things most apps don't:

**All-time highest rated** — your definitive, current #1 film based on your actual ratings.

**Recent highest rated (last 30 days)** — your current phase. Your taste shifts. Vista shows you what you're into right now, not just what you've loved forever.

**Favorite performer** — Vista analyzes every highly-rated film in your library and identifies which performer appears most often in your top films. You don't pick them — your ratings do.

Built for people who love film and want a cleaner, more personal record than the social-feed alternatives.
