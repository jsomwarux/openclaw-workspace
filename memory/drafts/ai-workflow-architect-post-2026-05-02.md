# Draft — LinkedIn/X: AI Workflow Architect Positioning

## Core idea
The market does not need more generic “AI automation consultants.” It needs people who can walk into a messy operation, identify one bottleneck, and turn it into a controlled workflow with measurable output.

## LinkedIn draft
Most SMBs do not need an AI strategy.

They need one broken workflow fixed.

A property manager does not care about “agentic transformation.”
They care that maintenance requests stop disappearing between tenants, vendors, and spreadsheets.

A distributor does not care about model benchmarks.
They care that reorder decisions are not trapped in one person’s inbox.

A contractor does not care about AI hype.
They care that estimate intake, follow-ups, and job updates stop eating half the day.

That is the real lane:

AI Workflow Architect.

Not “add AI everywhere.”

Map one workflow.
Find the bottleneck.
Build one controlled worker.
Measure the result.

One workflow.
One worker.
One business outcome.

That is how AI gets adopted by normal businesses.
