# Agent-Ready Revenue Layer / x402 Readiness Sprint — Draft Offer

## One-liner
I help API, data, and AI-product companies make their services agent-readable and agent-purchasable.

## Buyer
Companies with APIs, data products, paid reports, research tools, workflow endpoints, calculators, SaaS utilities, or AI tools.

## Problem
AI agents are starting to discover, call, and pay for services. Most products are not ready for that world. Their docs, pricing, outputs, receipts, and approval controls are built for humans clicking around, not agents completing tasks.

## Sprint Outcome
A clear answer to:
> Could an AI agent discover, evaluate, pay for, call, and cite this service?

## Deliverables
- Agent-readable service map
- Pay-per-use endpoint strategy
- Pricing model
- x402 feasibility check
- API/MCP/OpenAPI/llms.txt readiness checklist
- Receipt/audit/spend-control requirements
- Prototype endpoint recommendation or small scoped prototype

## What It Is Not
- Not generic crypto consulting
- Not wallet/custody/payment operations
- Not “install x402 everywhere”
- Not for offline SMBs without an agent-usable product surface

## Best CTA
“If you have an API, data product, or paid report and want to know whether agents could buy/use it, I can map the readiness layer.”
