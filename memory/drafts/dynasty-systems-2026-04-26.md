# Dynasty Systems Draft — 2026-04-26

Dynasty managers keep making the same math error:

they price rookie picks like scouting opinions are stable.

They aren't.

The 1.06 in April is a dream asset.
The 1.06 in October is usually one human being with:
- bad landing spot risk
- depth chart friction
- injury variance
- coaching uncertainty
- market patience decay

The exploit is not "draft better."

The exploit is understanding when the market values optionality more than outcomes.

Pre-draft capital is liquid.
Post-draft players are path-dependent.

That difference is where the edge lives.
