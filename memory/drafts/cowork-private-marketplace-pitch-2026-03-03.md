**Phase 1 — Discovery (Week 1)**
We interview 3–5 people on your team — the ones who do the actual work. We map the 5–10 workflows where time is being lost, information is siloed, or output quality is inconsistent. You get a written workflow audit with prioritized plugin opportunities before we write a single line of anything.

We design the plugin architecture for your top 3–5 use cases. Each plugin spec includes: what it does, what data it connects to, who uses it, and what the output looks like. You approve before build starts — no surprises.

**Phase 3 — Build & Deployment (Weeks 3–4)**
We build your private plugin marketplace using Anthropic's enterprise tooling. Plugins are configured, tested, and deployed to your Claude organization. Admin controls are set up so your team lead can manage access without calling us. Google Calendar and Gmail integrations are included by default.

**Phase 4 — Training & Handoff (Week 5–6)**
