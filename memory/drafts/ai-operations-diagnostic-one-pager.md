## What it is
A focused 2-week diagnostic for ops-heavy businesses that know AI matters but do not know what to build first.

The goal is not to sell a chatbot or a tool. The goal is to map the real workflow, identify the highest-ROI bottlenecks, and scope the first production AI/automation system.

## Best-fit buyers
- Family offices and property operators
- Construction and real estate teams
- Finance and back-office operations teams
- Hospitality and service businesses
- Any team running important work through email, spreadsheets, portals, local files, and human follow-up

## What it includes
1. **Workflow map** — how work actually moves today, including systems, people, handoffs, approvals, and failure points.
2. **Exception inventory** — what gets stuck, delayed, missed, duplicated, or escalated.
3. **Automation opportunity ranking** — 3–5 use cases scored by ROI, risk, complexity, data readiness, and owner visibility.
4. **Implementation roadmap** — what to build first, what to defer, and what should stay human-owned.
5. **First-build scope** — timeline, dependencies, acceptance criteria, integrations, handoff needs, and price range.

## Deliverables
- Current-state workflow map
- Bottleneck and exception list
- Use-case scoring matrix
- Recommended first build
- Implementation roadmap
- First-build proposal

## Timeline
2 weeks from kickoff.

## Positioning line
“Before you hire a full-time AI lead or buy another tool, this tells you what to build first and why.”

## Suggested pricing frame
Use case dependent. Present as a paid diagnostic that can be credited toward implementation if they proceed.

Suggested anchor range: **$3,500–$7,500**, depending on access, stakeholder count, and workflow complexity.
