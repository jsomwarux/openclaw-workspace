## Daily Protocol (30 min/day)
Do this like a human, not a bot. The goal is to train the algorithm, not to check boxes.

### Scroll behavior
- Open FYP and scroll naturally — watch some all the way through, skip others halfway
- Do NOT watch every video to completion. Real people don't do that.
- Watch videos that are relevant to crypto, game theory, finance, AI, trading

### Likes
- Like videos you'd actually like if this were your personal account
- Don't like every video. Don't like zero videos.
- Target: 5-15 genuine likes per 30-min session

### Following
- Follow 3-5 accounts in crypto/finance/game theory niche per session
- Examples: crypto analysis accounts, game theory creators, trading educators
- Don't mass-follow. Follow like a new user discovering the space.

### Comments
- Leave 2-4 genuine comments per session
- Not spam. Actual reactions to the content ("this aged well" / "missed this one" / "the Nash equilibrium angle here is underrated")
- Comments get you noticed by those creators' audiences

## Stop Signal
Open TikTok cold (don't force it). If the FYP is 80%+ crypto/finance/game theory content without you doing anything → warmup complete. Start posting.

## What Kills You
- Posting anything before warmup is complete
- Bot-like behavior (liking every video, following 100 accounts in one session)
- Posting AI-generated slides as your FIRST post (save that for week 2+, start with something human-feeling)

## Post-Warmup First Post Strategy
Week 1: 1-2 posts, simpler format (not a full 6-slide AI slideshow). Get a baseline engagement rate.
Week 2+: Full vibe marketing cron output — 6-slide slideshows, optimized hooks, full automation.
