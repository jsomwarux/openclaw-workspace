## Verdict
Adopt. The old system over-optimized research and contact completeness before enough buyers actually saw the message. The v2 system correctly prioritizes visibility, proof in M1, email deliverability, warm/referral asks, and measured volume.

## Superseded Rules
- Old rule: LinkedIn profile URL plus verified email required for send-ready.
- New rule: named buyer plus verified email OR accepted LinkedIn connection. Both are preferred, not mandatory.
- Old rule: proof mostly waits until M2.
- New rule: proof claim goes in M1.
- Old rule: curiosity CTAs only.
- New rule: concrete yes/no CTA is allowed when tied to visible proof.

## ICP Split
- 80% NYC metro property management firms, roughly 200-3,000 units, with stabilized/rent-regulated exposure or AppFolio/Yardi/Buildium signal.
- 20% NYC GCs and specialty trades, but only after the Job Status Dashboard proof asset exists.
- Trigger-only: wholesale distribution and Agentforce/insurance.

## Binary Gates
1. Live proof asset for the niche.
2. Reachable channel: verified email OR accepted LinkedIn connection.
3. Named buyer.
4. Trigger bonus: ops job posting, portfolio growth, PM software signal, stabilized exposure, or similar.

All four = T1. Gates 1-3 = T2. Anything less = hold/dead.

## Asset Cap
Build these three assets in order before more outbound asset work:
1. COI Compliance Autopilot proof pack.
2. Job Status Dashboard pack.
3. Arrears Follow-up one-pager.

No custom builds, decks, demos, or individual client folders before a first reply.

## PM Sequence
- Day 1: Email M1, proof claim in copy, no link.
- Day 2: LinkedIn connection request, blank or one non-pitch line.
- Day 4: LinkedIn DM if connected, one-pager PNG attached.
- Day 5: Email M2, Loom/artifact link delivered unconditionally.
- Day 10: Email M3, second angle.
- Day 18: Breakup plus referral ask. Recycle in 90 days.

## Measurement
Track per prospect: niche, tier, buyer title, channel, variant ID, touch dates, connection sent/accepted, email delivered/bounced, artifact views, reply/sentiment, and kill reason.

Do not judge a message variant below 30 sends. Do not judge a channel below 100 touches.
