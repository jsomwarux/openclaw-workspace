# Marketsmith Product-Team Discovery Plan

Created: 2026-05-08
Purpose: give JT a concrete product-team diagnostic structure if Marketsmith re-engages around MSIGenerate, msitheagency.com, or other AI/product initiatives.

## Positioning
Do **not** sell open-ended AI strategy or fractional hours first.

Sell a short product/AI diagnostic that gives the product team a concrete decision artifact:
- what workflow/user problem is worth solving,
- where AI changes the product or operating model,
- what the first buildable version should be,
- what risks/dependencies/acceptance criteria matter before committing to a bigger retainer.

## Recommended Offer
**Product/AI Diagnostic Sprint**

Suggested scope: 1–2 weeks, narrow to one initiative.

Best first initiatives:
1. MSIGenerate workflow/user journey.
2. msitheagency.com product/experience concept.
3. One internal marketing/product operations workflow where AI improves speed, personalization, QA, or throughput.

## Discovery Call Agenda — 45 Minutes
1. **Business objective** — what outcome matters: revenue, retention, speed, quality, personalization, cost, or internal throughput?
2. **Current workflow/user journey** — who starts it, what inputs exist, where handoffs happen, what breaks today?
3. **AI-native leverage point** — generation, classification, summarization, routing, retrieval, QA, personalization, or agentic workflow?
4. **Data boundary** — what data exists, where it lives, who can access it, what cannot leave the environment?
5. **Human approval** — what can AI draft/recommend vs. what must remain human-approved?
6. **First buildable version** — smallest useful version, inputs, outputs, UI/workflow surface, and acceptance criteria.
7. **Risk review** — hallucination, brand safety, legal/compliance, workflow ownership, user trust, data quality.
8. **Decision path** — who approves sprint, who owns product input, who validates output?

## Diagnostic Deliverable
5–8 page recommendation with:
- current-state workflow map,
- AI opportunity map,
- recommended first build,
- input/output contract,
- data and approval boundaries,
- build risks and dependencies,
- acceptance criteria,
- estimated effort/options for next phase.

## Qualification Rules
Proceed only if:
- product owner or strategy owner is involved,
- one initiative can be named,
- they want a decision artifact or build path, not vague advice,
- scope/authority is clear enough to avoid becoming low-leverage ad hoc consulting.

Pause or redirect if:
- they only want brainstorming,
- no product owner can attend,
- they want implementation without data/workflow ownership,
- budget/scope is unclear after the diagnostic framing.
