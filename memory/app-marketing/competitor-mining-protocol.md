# App Marketing OS — Competitor Mining Protocol
Date: 2026-05-19

## Purpose
Understand how similar apps get users and where users are frustrated, then convert gaps into product/marketing experiments.

## Monthly Required Teardown Per Active App
For each active app, inspect 5-8 competitors or adjacent products:
- App Store listing: title, subtitle, screenshots, description, reviews.
- Google Play listing when relevant.
- Website/landing page promise.
- SEO pages and comparison pages.
- TikTok/X/Reddit user behavior.
- Creator/newsletter/roundup presence.
- Share artifacts inside or around the product.

## Output Sections
1. Competitors inspected.
2. Acquisition channels visible.
3. User complaints and exact language.
4. Share loops/artifacts.
5. SEO/ASO patterns.
6. Borrowed audience opportunities.
7. Gaps JT can exploit.
8. Experiments created.

## App Baselines
- Vista: Letterboxd, Serializd, TV Time, Likewise, Sequel, JustWatch, IMDb.
- Nash Satoshi: CoinGecko categories, DeFiLlama, Token Terminal, Messari, crypto newsletters, AI-token dashboards.
- Glow Index: SkinSort, OnSkin, Yuka, INCIdecoder, Think Dirty, EWG Skin Deep, Sephora/Ulta review/search behavior.

## Rule
Do not claim a competitor strategy worked unless there is visible evidence: ranking, engagement, community behavior, backlinks, reviews, downloads estimate, or repeated user mention.
