Guardrail: JT sends all outreach. Eve drafts only. No financial advice, no price targets, no “buy/sell” claims.

## Outreach Principle
Do not pitch “please promote my app.” Pitch a useful asset:
> “I made a weekly ranking receipt graphic for [AI agents / DeFi / RWA] showing rank deltas, model disagreement, and what would change the thesis. Useful as a chart/snippet for your audience?”

Best first offer: **free embeddable Weekly Ranking Receipt Card** with methodology footer and no price predictions.

## Prioritization
Start with small/mid crypto media and X-native research accounts before massive outlets. The ideal target has:
- recurring newsletter/podcast/content cadence,
- appetite for charts/rankings/thesis debate,
- audience that understands narratives,
- low tolerance for obvious shills,
- credible but not impossible-to-reach distribution.
