## Page Copy

# A 1–100 movie rating app for people who need more than five stars

Vista is a movie rating app built for people who care about nuance.

Five stars are fine for quick reviews. But if you track movies seriously, the difference between “pretty good,” “great,” and “this is exactly my taste” often gets flattened.

Vista gives you a 1–100 rating scale so your movie ratings can be more precise, more personal, and more useful over time.

## Why five stars feel too blunt

A five-star scale forces too many movies into the same buckets.

Two films can both be four stars while meaning completely different things. One might be objectively well-made but not your taste. Another might be messy but unforgettable.

Movie taste is personal. A more precise scale helps capture that.

With Vista, you can rate a movie as a 72, an 86, or a 94 instead of squeezing everything into half-star jumps.

## What Vista helps you track

Vista helps you turn your watch history into a clearer taste profile.

You can use it to:

- rate movies from 1–100
- track what you have watched
- separate “good movie” from “good for me”
- notice patterns in your taste
- compare movie taste with friends where supported

The point is not just logging films. The point is understanding what your ratings say about you.

## Who Vista is for

Vista is for movie fans who already know that taste is part of the fun.

It is especially useful for:

- people who track every movie they watch
- Letterboxd-adjacent users who want more rating precision
- friends who compare movie taste
- people who want a personal film history, not just a public review feed
- anyone frustrated by blunt five-star ratings

## Why precision changes the way you rate movies

A 1–100 rating scale makes you think differently.

Instead of asking “Is this three and a half or four stars?” you can ask:

- How much did this actually work for me?
- Would I rate this higher than the last movie I watched?
- Is this a movie I respect, or a movie I love?
- Does my rating match my memory of it a week later?

That extra space makes your movie history more useful.

## FAQ

### Why use a 1–100 scale for movies?

A 1–100 scale gives you more room to express nuance. It helps separate movies that would otherwise get grouped into the same star rating.

### Is Vista a Letterboxd alternative?

Vista can be used alongside or instead of other movie apps, but its focus is different: precise personal ratings and movie taste as signal.

### Can I use Vista privately?

Vista is designed around personal movie tracking and taste. Use the app’s current privacy and social settings based on how you want to track and share ratings.

### Is Vista available on iPhone?

Vista is an iOS app. Use the App Store link on this page to check current availability.
