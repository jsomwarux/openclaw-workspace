## Short Description — 50 words
Nash Satoshi ranks crypto tokens using a 4-LLM ensemble that analyzes narrative strength, incentive design, coordination dynamics, and game-theory positioning. It is not price prediction. It is a structured way to compare how tokens are positioned before the market narrative gets crowded.

## Long Description — 150 words
Nash Satoshi is a crypto research tool that scores tokens through a multi-model AI analysis workflow. Instead of asking one model for a single opinion, Nash Satoshi uses multiple LLMs to evaluate each token across narrative, incentives, coordination dynamics, liquidity context, and game-theory positioning.

The goal is not to predict price. The goal is to expose where a token’s structure and story may be stronger or weaker than the current market conversation suggests.

Each ranking is built from model agreement and disagreement. When the models converge, that consensus becomes a signal. When they disagree, the disagreement itself becomes useful research context.

Nash Satoshi is for crypto traders and researchers who want to move beyond hype, influencer narratives, and single-model analysis toward a more structured view of token positioning.
