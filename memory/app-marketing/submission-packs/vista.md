## One-Line Tagline
A social movie-rating app for people who want more precision than five stars.

## Short Description — 50 words
Vista helps movie fans track what they watch, rate films on a precise 1–100 scale, and understand their taste beyond generic averages. It is built for people who treat movie taste as personal signal, not just a watchlist.

## Long Description — 150 words
Vista is a movie rating and tracking app built around personal taste. Instead of forcing every film into a blunt five-star system, Vista lets users rate movies from 1–100, build a clearer picture of their preferences, and compare taste with friends.

It is designed for movie fans who care about nuance: the difference between a 72 and an 86, the gap between “good” and “good for me,” and the patterns hiding inside years of watched films.

Vista is not trying to replace every social film platform. It is a more precise, personal layer for tracking what your movie history says about you. Rate films, build your taste profile, and use your watch history as a real signal instead of a messy list.
