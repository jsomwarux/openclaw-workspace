# Vista SEO Brief — 1–100 Movie Rating App

## Goal
Create a durable search/AI-search page for Vista around the measured winning positioning: rating precision.

## Target query
Primary: `1–100 movie rating app`

Secondary:
- precise movie rating app
- movie rating app with 100 point scale
- Letterboxd alternative precise ratings
- app to rate movies out of 100

## Search intent
People who feel 5 stars or half-stars are too blunt and want a more personal movie rating system.

## Page angle
Most movie apps ask whether a film was 3.5 or 4 stars. Vista asks for the number you actually mean.

## Direct answer block
A 1–100 movie rating app lets you score films with more precision than a 5-star or half-star scale. Vista uses a 100-point rating system so two movies that both feel like “4 stars” can still have different personal scores.

## Recommended URL
`/1-100-movie-rating-app`

Alternative if app routing is constrained:
`/movie-rating-app-100-point-scale`

## Page structure
1. H1: 1–100 movie rating app for people who hate vague star ratings
2. Direct-answer intro: define 1–100 movie rating and why it matters.
3. Problem section: 5 stars compress too much taste into too few choices.
4. Example section: “A movie can be technically 4 stars but emotionally 87.”
5. Vista section: how Vista handles precise ratings and taste profile.
6. Comparison section: 5-star ratings vs 1–100 ratings.
7. FAQ section.
8. App Store CTA.

## Copy blocks
### Hero
Five stars are too blunt.

Vista lets you rate movies from 1–100, so your ratings match what you actually felt.

### Problem
A 5-star scale turns too many different reactions into the same score.

A movie you barely liked can land next to a movie you still think about a week later.

That is useful for quick reviews. It is weak for personal taste.

### Example
Two movies can both be “4 stars.”

One might be an 81.
The other might be an 87.

That difference is where your taste actually lives.

### Vista explanation
Vista is built around precise personal ratings.

Instead of forcing every movie into stars, it lets you score films on a 1–100 scale and build a taste profile from your own ratings over time.

## FAQ
### Why rate movies out of 100?
Rating movies out of 100 gives you more precision than a 5-star scale. It helps separate movies that feel similar at a broad level but very different in personal taste.

### Is Vista a Letterboxd alternative?
Vista can be used as a more personal movie rating app, especially if you care more about precise ratings and taste tracking than public reviews or social feeds.

### Is a 100-point movie rating better than stars?
It depends on how you watch movies. Stars are faster. A 100-point scale is better when you want your ratings to reflect smaller differences between films.

### Can I track my movie taste over time?
Yes. Vista is designed to turn your personal movie ratings into a taste profile over time.
