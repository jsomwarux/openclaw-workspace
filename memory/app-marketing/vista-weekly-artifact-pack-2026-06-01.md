## Campaign Spine
Core promise:

Rate movies from 1-100. Vista turns your ratings into a taste profile worth sharing.

Primary audience:
- Letterboxd-adjacent movie fans
- iOS users who track watched movies
- people who think star ratings flatten their taste
- friend groups that compare recommendations, top fours, and hot takes

Primary CTA:
Rate 25 movies to make your taste card.

Secondary CTA:
Use 1-100 ratings when stars feel too blunt.

## Visual Card Concepts

### Card 1: Movie Taste Card
Format: square 1080x1080 and vertical 1080x1920

Modules:
- Vista logo/name
- "Movie Taste Card"
- sample user handle
- rating count: "25 movies rated"
- taste archetype: "Chaos With Standards"
- average score: `78/100`
- top four by actual rating
- taste tension line: "Five stars were hiding too much."
- footer CTA: "Rate 25 movies to make yours"

Design note:
Dark cinematic background, poster grid, one accent color. Let the big 1-100 numbers carry the differentiation.

### Card 2: 81 vs 87
Format: square card for X and Reddit image posts

Modules:
- headline: "Both are 4 stars. They are not the same rating."
- left movie placeholder: `81/100`
- right movie placeholder: `87/100`
- microcopy: "Vista is for the difference stars hide."
- CTA: "Rate movies with the number you mean"

Design note:
This is the clearest acquisition card for users who already understand star ratings.

### Card 3: Taste Clash Sample
Format: vertical 1080x1920

Modules:
- headline: "Same movie night. Different standards."
- sample pair:
  - Biggest agreement: `Movie A - both 91+`
  - Biggest disagreement: `Movie B - 92 vs 41`
- label: "sample compatibility card"
- CTA: "Compare your taste in Vista"

Design note:
Use only as a roadmap/feature-direction visual unless live comparison exists. Mark as sample to avoid fake product claims.

- If a post gets replies about "stars vs 100-point ratings," build more precision-scale content.
- If a post gets replies sharing top fours or taste identity jokes, build more taste-card content.
- If a post gets link clicks but no replies, keep the CTA and improve the visual.
- If a post gets low reach twice, do not increase volume. Fix asset clarity first.
