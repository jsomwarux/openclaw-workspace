# App Marketing OS — App Registry

## Status Values
- `active`: product is live enough for ongoing marketing.
- `prelaunch`: product is not live but should build audience/waitlist.
- `pending`: product exists but marketing should wait for a blocker to clear.
- `paused`: product should not receive routine marketing.
- `idea`: passive-income idea not yet selected for build.

## Action Arena
Status: prelaunch / priority build

What it is:
- Fantasy football league structure + sports betting strategy.
- Players join leagues.
- Each player receives a weekly fake budget, default `$100`.
- Players compete on profit through straight bets, parlays, and teasers.
- No real money is ever wagered.

Primary audience:
- Fantasy football players who like league competition and trash talk.
- Sports bettors who want bankroll/card-construction discipline without real-money risk.
- Group chats that already argue about picks and parlays.

Primary channel:
- @dynastyjig native sports betting/fantasy strategy content.

Secondary channels:
- Waitlist/prelaunch page.
- SEO pages: fake-money sports betting league, fantasy football betting game, betting strategy league, parlay strategy game.
- Reddit strategy prompts only if compliant.

Current systems:
- Sports GM skill already has Action Arena positioning.
- Daily @dynastyjig content uses Action Arena as invisible backdrop.
- Not yet in Vibe Marketing product registry.

Recommended posture:
- Do not add to generic Vibe Marketing until there is a landing/waitlist page and launch assets.
- Build demand through native strategy content first.

Weekly metric:
- @dynastyjig engagement.
- Waitlist signups once live.
- Beta league interest.
- Number of qualified beta users.

Promotion boundary:
- No real-money wagering language.
- No gambling promises.
- Emphasize fake budget, strategy, competition, accountability.
