# App Marketing OS — Revised Operating Model
Date: 2026-05-19

## Verdict
The app marketing strategy must optimize for repeatable niche-specific acquisition loops, not generic content ideas. Each app needs a product-led share artifact, a measurement spine, competitor/review mining, and borrowed-audience distribution.

## Critical Upgrades
1. Product-led share loops are mandatory.
   - Vista: movie taste card, compatibility score, top-four/taste identity card, watchlist screenshot.
   - Glow Index: routine/product verdict card, ingredient/suitability card, ranked product screenshot.
   - Nash Satoshi: daily ranking card, undervalued/overhyped leaderboard, weekly receipt card.

2. Every experiment needs tracking.
   - UTM/deep link or source tag.
   - Post/source URL.
   - Creative type.
   - Audience.
   - 24h/72h/7d result.
   - Downloads, signups, clicks, search impressions, or ranking movement where available.

3. Borrowed audience is a top channel, not a side tactic.
   Build app-specific lists of micro-creators, niche newsletters, subreddit/community mods, roundup writers, app reviewers, TikTok demo accounts, Discord/Telegram owners, podcast hosts, and affiliate/review sites.

4. Competitor mining becomes mandatory.
   Mine App Store reviews, Google Play reviews where relevant, Reddit complaints, TikTok comments, Product Hunt comments, SEO pages, comparison pages, and competitor onboarding/screenshots.

## Niche Acquisition Laws

### Vista
Do not compete with Letterboxd as a generic movie logger. Wedge: taste identity and compatibility.
Research: Letterboxd, Serializd, TV Time, Likewise, Sequel, JustWatch, r/Letterboxd, r/MovieSuggestions, FilmTok, Film Twitter, top-four movie meme behavior.
Winning patterns: friend/follower loops, identity sharing, taste cards, recap/list formats, compatibility hooks.

### Nash Satoshi
Do not market as another crypto dashboard. Wedge: ranking thesis plus receipts.
Research: Messari-style research, Token Terminal, DeFiLlama, CoinGecko category pages, crypto newsletters, analyst accounts, Telegram/Discord investor communities, undervalued-token threads.
Winning patterns: daily/weekly ranking deltas, transparent methodology, contrarian calls with receipts, shareable charts/cards, small newsletter/account partnerships.

### Glow Index
Do not treat as a launch-only app. Wedge: search + shopping decision support.
Research: SkinSort, OnSkin, Yuka, INCIdecoder, Think Dirty, EWG Skin Deep, Sephora/Ulta review patterns, r/SkincareAddiction, r/AsianBeauty, TikTok scan-before-buy creators.
Winning patterns: product database SEO, ingredient explainers, creator shopping demos, routine/report cards, “safe for sensitive/acne-prone skin?” decision hooks. Avoid medical claims.

## Winning Pattern Research Pipeline
Daily light scan: collect high-signal examples from TikTok, X, Reddit, App Store reviews, competitor pages, SEO results, newsletters, and YouTube when useful.
Weekly synthesis: score patterns and turn only the best into experiments.
Monthly teardown: deep competitor review by app/niche.

Pattern scoring, 1-5 each:
- Audience fit
- Repeatability
- Proof of traction
- Cost fit under $0-$200/mo
- Conversion intent
- Shareability
- Competition gap

Only 24+/35 patterns become Mission Control experiments.

## Channel Weighting
1. Product-led share artifacts — highest priority.
2. Borrowed audience — highest low-budget external lever.
3. Search/ASO/pSEO — compounding discovery.
4. Native short-form — useful only when format matches niche behavior.
5. Reddit/community — high signal if value-first and non-spammy.
6. Directories — useful for backlinks and surface area, not standalone traction.
7. Product Hunt — optional launch burst, not a primary acquisition engine.
