# App Marketing OS — Borrowed Audience Playbook
Date: 2026-05-19

## Purpose
Borrowed audience is a primary low-budget channel. The goal is to place useful app-native assets in front of existing communities and creators, not beg for promotion.

## Target Types
- Micro-creators: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, IG Reels, X.
- Newsletter writers.
- Roundup/comparison article authors.
- App reviewers and directory owners.
- Subreddit/community moderators.
- Discord/Telegram owners.
- Podcast/newsletter hosts.
- Affiliate/review sites where safe.

## Pitch Rules
- JT sends all external outreach.
- Eve drafts and saves; never sends.
- Lead with a useful asset or angle for their audience.
- Do not ask creators to “promote my app.” Ask if a specific artifact/useful comparison would be interesting.

## App Angles
- Vista: taste cards, rating debates, movie-list templates, creator compatibility prompts.
- Nash: weekly ranking receipt, model-disagreement chart, thesis/methodology graphic.
- Glow: product verdict card, safe shopping demo, ingredient/value comparison. No medical claims.
