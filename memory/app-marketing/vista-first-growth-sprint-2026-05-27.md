# Vista-First App Growth Sprint — 2026-05-27

## Decision
Run the next app-marketing sprint as a Vista-first, artifact-led growth test. Grok's live-X read supports visual taste/ranking content, but the operating model should be data-led artifacts rather than founder-influencer posting.

## Portfolio Allocation
- Vista: 60-70% of app-marketing slots.
- Nash Satoshi: 20-30%, focused on ranking receipts and methodology-backed posts.
- Glow Index: SEO/AI-search first; no social-volume push until crawler, claim-safety, and measurement paths are healthy.

## Weekly Cadence
- 5-7 total app posts per week across the portfolio.
- Vista gets 3-4 posts.
- Nash gets 1-2 posts.
- Glow gets SEO/page work first, not recurring X/Reddit volume.
- Reddit stays manual and community-native only. No automated Reddit cross-posting.

## Vista Formats
1. Most divisive movies this week.
2. Your 1-100 rating says this about your taste.
3. Taste match / taste clash comparison card.

## Automation Rule
Automate artifact production, not personality.

Automate:
- screenshots and visual cards;
- caption drafts;
- weekly queue creation;
- SEO/page refresh candidates;
- metric capture and next-week recommendations.

Keep manual:
- final X approval/posting;
- replies;
- Reddit submissions;
- launch-style posts;
- anything involving skincare or financial claims.

## Guardrails
- Do not post 4-5 times per week per app.
- Do not run automated Reddit posting.
- Do not use Glow before/after, medical, treatment, or fake-testimonial framing.
- Do not use Nash as financial advice, price prediction, or return framing.
- Do not let app marketing outrank Altmark/consulting proof work.

## First Execution Batch
1. Build a Vista-first weekly artifact pack: 5 draft posts, 3 visual card concepts, source tags, and CTAs.
2. Scope a reusable post-card generator for Vista first, then adapt to Nash and Glow.
3. Build a Vista SEO/AI-search page map around movie taste, 1-100 rating, and taste comparison queries.
4. Add a weekly app signal review that recommends the next 5-7 posts from metrics, not guesses.
