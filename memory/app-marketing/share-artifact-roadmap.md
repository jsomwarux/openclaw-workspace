# App Marketing OS — Product-Led Share Artifact Roadmap
Date: 2026-05-19

## Rule
Every consumer app needs at least one share artifact that makes the user look smart, funny, tasteful, early, or correct. If the product cannot generate a shareable artifact, external marketing will be weaker.

## Vista
Primary artifact: **Movie Taste Card**
- User's rating style / taste profile.
- Top movies or most polarizing ratings.
- Optional compatibility score with a friend.
- Export: 9:16 and square image.
- CTA: “Compare your taste” / “Rate 100 movies.”

Secondary artifacts:
- “My top 4 by actual rating.”
- “Movies I rate higher/lower than everyone else.”
- Watchlist / recent ratings screenshot.

## Nash Satoshi
Primary artifact: **Weekly Ranking Receipt Card**
- Top movers.
- Model disagreement.
- Undervalued/overhyped leaderboard.
- Methodology note: game theory / narrative / incentive analysis, not price prediction.
- Export: X-native image and 9:16 slideshow cover.

Secondary artifacts:
- Token detail receipt.
- “Four models disagreed most on...” card.
- Weekly rank delta chart.

## Glow Index
Primary artifact: **Product Verdict Card**
- Product name.
- Score/rank.
- Why it scored that way: formula/value/evidence/marketing-claim check.
- Safe suitability language only.
- Export: Pinterest/TikTok/IG-friendly.

Secondary artifacts:
- Routine report card.
- Category leaderboard card.
- Ingredient/value explainer card.

## Guardrails
- Glow: no medical/dermatology/treatment claims, fake testimonials, fake before/after, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Nash: no returns/price predictions/financial advice.
- Vista: do not position as a universal Letterboxd replacement; lead with precision/taste identity.
