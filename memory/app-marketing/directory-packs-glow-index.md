## 150-word description

Glow Index is an AI-powered skincare product research site that ranks and compares skincare products across six practical dimensions: ingredient efficacy, safety profile, value for money, formula transparency, skin compatibility, and sensory usability.

Instead of relying on brand marketing copy, influencer hype, or one AI answer, Glow Index uses a multi-model analysis process to surface consensus, disagreement, score breakdowns, and reasoning where available. The goal is to help shoppers understand how a formula is positioned, where the value is, and which claims deserve a closer look.

Glow Index is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent skin conditions. It is a consumer research tool for comparing skincare products, pricing, formulas, and marketing claims before buying.
