## 150-word description
Vista is a movie rating app for people who think five stars are too blunt.

Most movie apps compress too many different reactions into the same rating. Vista gives film fans a 1–100 rating scale so they can separate movies that are both “good” but not good in the same way.

Use Vista to rate movies with more precision, track what you have watched, and build a clearer picture of your personal taste over time. It is especially useful for people who want to distinguish between “good movie” and “good for me.”

Vista is not trying to replace every film platform. It is a more precise personal rating layer for movie fans who want their ratings to say more.
