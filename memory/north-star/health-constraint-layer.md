# Health Constraint Layer

Purpose: treat JT's sleep, health, and energy as economic infrastructure, not soft side quests.

## Operating Thesis
If JT feels bad, all financial lanes degrade. Health is the base layer for consulting sales, app distribution, crypto judgment, and content consistency.

## Inputs
- Health check-ins / health SQLite if available
- Workout reports
- User-stated sleep, energy, stress, illness, soreness, burnout
- Workload and task volume
- Upcoming high-cognitive-load tasks

## Daily Routing Rules
- High energy / good sleep: allow sales, deep work, content, and strategic decisions.
- Medium energy: prioritize cash-critical tasks and small distribution actions; avoid heavy optional builds.
- Low energy / poor sleep / sickness: protect recovery; only urgent cash/client obligations and lightweight admin.
- Stress spike: reduce context-switching and pick one clear next action.

## Weekly Output
- Health/focus risk for the week
- Workload adjustment recommendation
- One thing to defer if energy is low
- One health action that protects earning capacity
- Max recommended deep-work load for the week: normal / reduced / minimum viable
- Tasks to protect even on low-energy weeks: client obligations, high-probability revenue, health basics

## Guardrails
- Do not moralize health.
- Do not recommend sacrificing sleep for momentum.
- Do not pile on extra work when health signal is poor.
- Treat recurring bad sleep/low energy as a strategic blocker.

## Integration With North Star
The Weekly North Star Command Center should downgrade optional project work when health/energy risk is high and should protect consulting/client essentials first.
