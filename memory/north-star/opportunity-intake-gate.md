# Opportunity Intake Gate

Purpose: prevent every interesting opportunity from becoming work.

## Operating Thesis
JT's bottleneck is not ideas. It is focus, distribution, cash flow, and energy. Opportunities must earn their way into the system.

## Applies To
- New app ideas
- New consulting niches
- New tools/plugins/APIs
- Job opportunities
- Crypto theses
- Content lanes
- Partnerships
- New automations/agents

## Scoring Criteria
Score 1-5:
1. Cash-flow potential
2. Distribution advantage or audience fit
3. Reusable IP / product leverage
4. Fit with JT's AI implementation consultant + product builder identity
5. Energy/focus cost, inverted: 5 = low burden
6. Timing urgency or asymmetric upside

Total possible: 30.

## Decision Rules
- 24-30: Promote to active exploration or task
- 18-23: Save as future signal with trigger condition
- 12-17: Defer unless it unblocks active priority
- <12: Ignore/prune

## Required Fields For Any New Opportunity
- What it is
- Why now
- Who it serves
- How it could make money or create leverage
- Distribution path
- Smallest validation step
- Kill condition
- Trigger condition if deferred

## Guardrails
- Do not add vague ideas to Mission Control.
- Do not create speculative tasks without a concrete first action and done state.
- If opportunity is attractive but not now, write it to `memory/future-signals.md` with a trigger condition.
- If it risks pulling JT away from consulting cash flow without clear upside, recommend defer.
- Do not promote more than 3 new opportunities per week unless one is time-sensitive and clearly high-upside.
- Every promoted opportunity must name what gets deprioritized or why it fits inside current capacity.

## Acquisition Opportunity Gate — Dead SaaS → Agent Company
Dead/stalled SaaS acquisitions are validation-only unless they pass strict gates. Do not recommend buying an asset just because the thesis is compelling.

Required before promotion:
- First experiment acquisition cost ≤ $5K unless JT explicitly approves higher.
- Legal transfer of code, domain/brand, customer list, analytics, support history, and relevant IP is clear.
- Privacy/data rights permit analysis and compliant outreach/ads. Never assume old customer data can be uploaded to ad platforms.
- Support tickets or customer conversations show repeated workflow pain that an agent can execute, not just a dashboard feature request list.
- Distribution path exists before rebuild.
- Maintenance burden is low enough not to compete with consulting cash flow.

Default smallest step: create a 20-candidate scanner/research list and score candidates before founder outreach. Reference: `memory/opportunities/dead-saas-agent-acquisition.md`.
