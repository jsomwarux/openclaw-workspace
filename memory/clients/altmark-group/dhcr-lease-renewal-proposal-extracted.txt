AutomationAdd-On Proposal
Prepared for

SCOPE
Phase 1: Legal Rent Renewals Only $3,500
Covers ~2/3 of stabilized units (~40 leases). Preferential rent renewals scoped as Phase 2.
Daily automated scan of the command center spreadsheet for upcoming lease expirations
At 120 days before expiration: generates a completed RTP-8 Renewal Lease Form as a filled PDF
Calculates 1-year and 2-year renewal rent options using configurable RGB percentages, including
support for stacked 2-year increases
Calculates additional security deposit required for each renewal option
Populates all tenant information, owner/entity information (entity name varies by property, address
always 2447 Third Ave, c/o Matt Grabina), lease dates, and computed fields
Emails completed forms to Matt with a summary of all renewals generated that day
At 95 days before expiration: sends a reminder for any forms not yet marked as sent, with the 90-
day statutory deadline highlighted
Weekly summary digest: all upcoming expirations in the next 150 days, form status, and deadline
tracking
Tracks all renewal activity in Google Sheets for audit and visibility
DATA SOURCE
Command Center Spreadsheet
Single source of truth maintained by Matt, combining DHCR Rent Roll and current rent roll data.
The spreadsheet includes: entity name, property address, apartment number, tenant name, apartment
status, legal registered rent, current security deposit on file, lease start date, and lease end date. The
workflow reads from this spreadsheet and writes tracking columns back to it (form generated date,
form sent date). 
RENT CALCULATION LOGIC
Automated Computations
1-year renewal: Legal Rent × (1 + 1-year RGB rate)
2-year renewal (standard): Legal Rent × (1 + 2-year RGB rate)

2-year renewal (stacked): Legal Rent × (1 + year 1 rate) × (1 + year 2 rate) — for split-year RGB
increases
Additional security deposit: New legal rent minus current deposit on file
RGB rates are configurable — Matt updates once per year. Current rates: 3.0% (1-year), 4.5% (2-
year)
DELIVERY & REVIEW
What Matt Handles
The workflow generates the forms and manages timing. Matt reviews each form for accuracy, prints
and mails to tenants, countersigns returned forms, and updates tenant accounts in AppFolio. The
workflow does not send forms directly to tenants or interact with AppFolio. 
FUTURE EXPANSION
Phase 2: Preferential Rent Renewals
Scoped and priced separately after Phase 1 is live and validated.
Adds support for the remaining ~1/3 of stabilized units with preferential rents. Requires additional form
fields, dual rent calculations (legal vs. preferential), and a supplemental form.
