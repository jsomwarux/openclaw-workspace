## Offer

Ops Copilot Retainer: $1,500/month

Proposal status: draft price only. Not accepted, not invoiced, and not active unless JT chooses to present it after rent delinquency acceptance.

Purpose: keep the live Altmark workflow environment stable, monitored, and improving without turning every small adjustment into a separate project.

## Included Each Month

1. Healthchecks monitoring
   - Confirm scheduled workflows are running.
   - Review logs for failed runs, stale inputs, missing exports, and abnormal queue counts.
   - Send a short status summary with open issues and owner/date.

2. Fixes
   - Resolve small workflow issues, broken mappings, copy/config updates, and source-format changes.
   - Keep rollback/manual fallback documented for sensitive workflows.
   - Maintain the Client OS runbooks and acceptance checklists.

3. One enhancement/month
   - One scoped improvement to an existing workflow.
   - Examples: add a new exception reason, adjust reviewer queue layout, add summary email, improve owner report, add a new field mapping, or tune reminder cadence.

## Boundaries

Included:
- Monitoring and support for workflows already built.
- Small fixes and one scoped enhancement/month.
- Review-only queues, logs, summaries, and human approval gates.

Not included:
- New standalone workflows.
- Tenant-facing sends without explicit approval.
- Financial transactions, transfers, or accounting writebacks without human review.
- Legal judgment or eviction decisioning.
- Major rebuilds caused by a new source system or new scope.

## Why It Matters

Altmark's workflows touch sensitive property and finance operations. The value is not just the build; it is keeping the system trustworthy after handoff:
- failures are visible
- stale source data is caught
- exception queues stay clean
- staff know what needs review
- small improvements do not wait for a new project cycle
