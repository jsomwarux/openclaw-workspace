## What This Demonstrates
- Local-first AI/automation for sensitive family-office or property-operations workflows.
- Automation around existing systems instead of forced SaaS replacement.
- Exception dashboard thinking: surface stuck/expired/missing/high-risk items rather than letting work disappear in spreadsheets.
- Human-in-the-loop design for financial, tenant-facing, or sensitive actions.
- Audit logs and acceptance criteria so the client knows what changed and what is live.
