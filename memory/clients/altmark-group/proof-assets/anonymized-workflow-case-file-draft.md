## Safe Default Positioning

**Local-first automation infrastructure for a NYC family-office/property-operations team.**

## Problem

Property/family-office operations often have recurring compliance and reporting workflows spread across files, inboxes, and manual follow-up. The risk is not just time spent. It is missed expirations, unclear ownership, weak audit trails, and sensitive data moving through tools that may not fit the client’s privacy posture.

## Constraints

- Sensitive property/financial operations data should stay local where possible.
- Human approval remains required for external or financial actions.
- The workflow needs clear admin/access ownership.
- The system must be understandable enough for nontechnical operators to trust.
- Proof must not expose tenant, vendor, property, financial, or client-private details.
