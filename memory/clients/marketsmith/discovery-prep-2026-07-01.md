# Marketsmith Discovery Prep - 2026-07-01

## Source
Sam emailed JT that Marketsmith is expediting dashboard creation and AI-generated insights. Team is building in Azure and using vibe coding. Need 10-15 priority dashboards in 30 days with one active builder plus data engineering support for pipelines, cleansing, and organized inputs. Team members Amanda, David, and Ed may meet with JT. Pricing likely project basis for 3-4 weeks.

## Fit Read
Strong fit if scoped as analytics product delivery and AI insight workflow design, not as raw engineering staff augmentation.

JT's edge:
- Translate business questions into dashboard requirements and acceptance criteria.
- Create repeatable dashboard specs, review workflows, and prioritization.
- Define AI-generated insight patterns, guardrails, and human review loops.
- Coordinate with data engineering without pretending to own the pipeline layer.
- Turn vibe-coded dashboards into a governed delivery system.

Risk:
- 10-15 dashboards in 30 days is aggressive.
- If they expect one person to own Azure engineering, data modeling, QA, stakeholder management, and dashboard build execution, scope will sprawl.
- Pricing must reflect urgency, access to data team, and expected weekly output.

## Recommended Offer Shape
Position as a 30-day dashboard acceleration sprint:
- Week 1: intake, dashboard inventory, priority scoring, reusable dashboard template/spec, first 1-2 builds or prototypes.
- Weeks 2-3: production build/review rhythm, AI-generated insight framework, stakeholder demos, quality control.
- Week 4: final polish, handoff, backlog, reusable operating playbook.

Best pricing posture:
- Floor: $12,000 for a tightly scoped advisory/build-support sprint.
- Recommended anchor: $18,000-$22,500 for 3-4 weeks, assuming active delivery support, dashboard specs, QA/review loop, and AI insight framework.
- Premium/high-intensity: $25,000-$30,000 if JT is expected to be embedded, drive delivery cadence, coordinate stakeholders, and materially unblock 10-15 dashboards.

Avoid hourly framing unless they force it. If hourly is unavoidable, anchor $175-$225/hr with a weekly minimum.

## Meeting Prep Questions
1. What are the 10-15 dashboards, and who is the decision owner for each?
2. Which dashboards are must-ship in 30 days versus nice-to-have?
3. What stack is being used in Azure and for visualization?
4. What exactly does "AI-generated insights" mean: narrative summaries, anomaly flags, recommendations, explanations, or automated actions?
5. What data is already clean, available, and modeled versus still in pipeline work?
6. What does done mean for each dashboard: prototype, internal demo, production use, or executive-ready?
7. Who approves UX, metrics definitions, and insight accuracy?
8. What is the expected role split between JT, the active builder, and data engineering?
9. What existing examples should JT mirror or improve?
10. Are they looking for dashboard production, a reusable dashboard factory, or both?

## Initial Recommendation
Pursue. This aligns with JT's operator-builder skillset and consulting positioning if he controls scope around dashboard delivery systems, requirements, AI insight design, and stakeholder-ready outputs. Do not sell as pure Azure developer capacity.
