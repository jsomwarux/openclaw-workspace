# Agentic Marketing + Insurance Ops Signal — 2026-06-05

## Fresh Search Check
- Ran direct Brave searches with `scripts/web_search.py` on 2026-06-05 for Agentforce Marketing, insurance underwriting AI governance, and NYC/small-business AI workflow automation.
- No public outreach or posting was done.

## What Surfaced
- Salesforce framed Agentforce Marketing as an agentic marketing layer where agents can help with campaign workflows, content generation, localization, segmentation, and personalization when connected to customer context.
- The useful Salesforce detail is not "AI writes copy"; it is the operational dependency: agents need customer data, workflow context, approvals, and handoff rules to avoid disconnected customer actions.
- Insurance underwriting search results keep showing an adoption/confidence gap. Insurance Business reported nearly 90% of respondents expect more underwriting tasks to be automated, while 65.9% plan to introduce additional tools in 2026.
- Governance remains the buyer blocker. ProgramBusiness/Grant Thornton coverage pointed to insurers reporting AI revenue gains while struggling to prove governance controls.
- NYC/small-business search results point toward workflow-level automation: guest complaint triage, booking workflows, bottleneck mapping, and agents acting in existing tools.

## JT Move
- Package this as "agentic workflow readiness" rather than generic AI implementation.
- For marketing/property/local-business prospects: lead with distribution workflow mapping, customer-context hygiene, approval gates, and exception handoff.
- For insurance/regulated prospects: lead with underwriting workflow inventory, governance evidence, human-review boundaries, vendor/source-of-truth mapping, and audit logs.
- Altmark proof can be framed as the same operating model: workflow intake -> rules and context -> governed agent action -> human escalation -> measurable outcome.

## Safe Offer Language
- "I map the workflow before adding the agent."
- "The value is not generated copy; it is the handoff system around customer context, approvals, and exceptions."
- "For regulated teams, the first deliverable is proof that the system knows when not to act."
