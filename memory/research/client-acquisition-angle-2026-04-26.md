## 6PM Addendum — Wholesale Distribution Wedge

Recent distribution/ERP coverage keeps pointing at AI value in operational edges: inventory questions, order status, customer service, field service, logistics, and ERP-adjacent execution.

**Wholesale wedge:** many distributors do not need a full ERP transformation first. They need a front-office agent that can answer/triage repetitive customer requests using existing ERP exports, inbox history, and inventory/status spreadsheets.

**Prototype flow:**
- Customer email asks for stock/lead time/order status.
- Agent classifies intent: quote, stock check, ETA, backorder, return, invoice, technical question.
- Agent drafts reply from inventory/order data and flags exceptions.
- Human approves before send.

**Pitch line:**
"If your team answers the same stock, ETA, and order-status questions every day, I can prototype an approval-first AI assistant that drafts those replies from your existing data before we touch the ERP."
