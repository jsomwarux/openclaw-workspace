## Ranked Opportunities

### 1) Oil & Gas Supplier Ops Copilot — GO
**Buyer:** Guyanese/local-content-certified suppliers, logistics firms, safety/compliance vendors, manpower firms.  
**Pain:** certification paperwork, quote tracking, local-content evidence, supplier onboarding, bid readiness, invoice/document chasing.  
**Offer:** $2.5K–$7.5K workflow audit + automation sprint: document intake, compliance checklist, tender tracker, CRM/vendor database, WhatsApp/email routing.  
**Why JT fits:** business-ops + AI implementation credibility; no need to sell as a developer.

### 2) Diaspora Business Network Intelligence Brief — HOLD/TEST
**Buyer:** Guyanese diaspora investors/operators evaluating market entry.  
**Pain:** fragmented info, unclear procurement/local partner landscape, difficulty tracking real opportunities.  
**Offer:** paid monthly brief or private research package.  
**Risk:** could become content/research treadmill without conversion. Test with 3–5 warm diaspora contacts before building.

### 3) Government Tender Automation / Bid Desk — HOLD
**Buyer:** SMEs bidding on NPTA tenders.  
**Pain:** tracking tenders, eligibility, deadlines, document packs.  
**Why hold:** procurement workflows are real, but cold trust barrier is high and direct government work is slow. Needs local partner first.
