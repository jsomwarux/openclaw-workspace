# Local Content Operations Sprint — Guyana Offer

## One-line offer
A 2–3 week workflow audit and automation sprint for Guyana-connected oil/gas suppliers dealing with local-content certification, tender tracking, vendor documents, bid readiness, and admin drag.

## Who it is for
- Local-content-certified Guyanese suppliers
- Oil/gas logistics and marine support firms
- Safety/compliance vendors
- Staffing/manpower providers
- Procurement and service contractors
- Construction/support firms serving oil/gas or public infrastructure
- Local tech/consulting firms that need an AI workflow specialist as a partner

## Buyer pain
Growth is creating more opportunity, but also more admin load:
- certification and renewal docs;
- supplier registration requirements;
- tender/bid tracking;
- contract and compliance evidence;
- quote/invoice follow-up;
- WhatsApp/email document sprawl;
- vendor/customer records living across inboxes and spreadsheets;
- no clear owner for next steps.

## What the sprint includes
1. **Workflow map** — current intake → document → approval → follow-up process.
2. **Compliance/document checklist** — required fields, owners, due dates, renewal dates.
3. **Bid/tender tracker** — opportunity, deadline, status, owner, document pack status.
4. **Vendor/customer CRM lite** — key contacts, category, status, last touch, next action.
5. **Document intake system** — folder structure + naming convention + intake form.
6. **Automation layer** — n8n where useful: reminders, status updates, handoffs, dashboard refresh.
7. **Exception dashboard** — overdue docs, aging bids, missing fields, pending approvals.
8. **Runbook** — how staff use and maintain the system.

## Deliverables
- Current-state workflow diagram
- Future-state workflow diagram
- Working tracker/dashboard
- Automation rules where appropriate
- SOP/runbook
- 30-day improvement roadmap
