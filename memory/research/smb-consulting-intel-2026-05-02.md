# SMB Consulting Intel — 2026-05-02 Heartbeat

## Quick signal
Recent search results continue to point toward the same wedge: SMB owners do not need generic “AI transformation”; they need small operational workers that remove one recurring bottleneck.

## Useful angles for JT
- **Property management:** maintenance triage + dispatch remains the clearest demo lane. The buyer pain is concrete: tenant request intake, vendor routing, response SLAs, and follow-up tracking.
- **Wholesale/distribution:** “Inventory Worker” framing is promising: prevented stockouts, waste reduction, reorder accuracy, and quote/order follow-up are measurable outcomes.
- **Construction/trades:** best angle is not futuristic AI; it is job admin automation — estimate intake, scheduling, procurement follow-up, project status updates, and customer routing.

## Outreach implication
Lead with a 7-Day Operational Audit around one workflow, not “AI agents.” Suggested opener angle: “I map the one workflow leaking the most admin time, then build a small internal worker around it.”

## Content implication
A strong LinkedIn post could compare three SMB “AI worker” use cases:
1. Maintenance Triage Worker for property managers
2. Inventory/Reorder Worker for distributors
3. Estimate Intake Worker for contractors
