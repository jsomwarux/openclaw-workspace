# NYCB Contributor Prospect Pilot - 2026-06-03

## Critic Verdict

### 1. What JT is Actually Saying
JT is not really saying "this donor list is a lead list." He is saying: "I was in a room where money, taste, status, and NYC operating networks overlap. Can I turn that proximity into pipeline?"

That instinct is good. The sloppy version is assuming wealth implies a business pain worth pitching. Wealth alone is not an ICP.

### 2. Broken Reasoning / True Constraint
The broken assumption is: family contributor -> wealthy family -> owns outdated business -> good AI consulting prospect.

That breaks because many names are foundations, public agencies, mega-brands, pure patrons, or people whose wealth is not attached to an accessible operating company. The true constraint is not whether the list is rich enough. The true constraint is whether each name can be enriched into:

- an actual operating business
- a reachable operator or buyer
- a visible workflow/admin burden
- a proof-safe reason JT can speak to them without referencing the donor-list source

### 3. Avoidance / Cost
The risk is using the list as a way to feel like prospecting while staying one step away from actual outreach.

If this becomes "research wealthy families for hours," it is avoidance. If it becomes a 25-name enrichment sprint with a hard pass/fail gate, it is pipeline.

Cost of doing it wrong: 3-5 hours spent on status names with no buyer path.
Cost of not testing it: losing a potentially useful NYC cultural-network signal that could surface family-owned operating companies.

### 4. What a Stronger Operator Would Do
They would not debate the whole list. They would run a small extraction test:

1. OCR 25 visible names.
2. Remove foundations, public agencies, mega-brands, anonymous donors, and celebrities.
3. Enrich only names tied to operating companies in NYC/NJ/CT.
4. Score for proof fit, workflow pain, buyer access, and contact completeness.
5. Produce either 5 qualified prospect briefs or kill the channel.

The one thing to act on this week: run the 50-name enrichment pilot and stop if it does not produce at least 5 prospects scoring 60+ with a real company and contact route.

## Pass / Fail

Pass as a research signal.

Fail as a direct outreach list.

Never mention NYCB, ballet, donor status, or contributor tiers in outreach. The public donor list can guide enrichment, but the outreach angle must be operating pain, local business context, or referral/network relevance.

## Pilot Rules

### Include

- Family-owned or founder-led operating businesses
- NYC/NJ/CT businesses with admin-heavy workflows
- Real estate, property management, construction, logistics, facilities, insurance/admin, healthcare/admin, professional services, hospitality, private-market operations
- Companies where JT's proof maps cleanly to documents, approvals, reporting, vendor/customer follow-up, compliance, renewals, tenant/vendor workflows, dashboards, or staff handoff

### Exclude

- Anonymous entries
- Pure foundations or donor-advised vehicles with no operating company
- Public agencies or elected officials
- Celebrities where the path is personal brand, not operations
- Mega-brands unless a specific SMB/vendor/subsidiary operating wedge appears
- Anyone without LinkedIn + verified email before send-ready status

## Scoring Gate

Use the consulting pipeline tier rubric, with a channel-specific prefilter:

- Operating company confirmed: required
- NYC/metro or remote-serviceable: required
- Workflow pain visible: required
- Buyer/contact path: required before outreach
- LinkedIn + verified email: required before send-ready

Scoring:

- 25 proof proximity: Altmark/Aya/property/construction/admin resemblance
- 20 revenue likelihood: realistic $3.5K-$15K project
- 15 workflow specificity: concrete docs/approvals/reporting/follow-up/compliance burden
- 15 buyer/channel quality: decision-maker, warm route, verified email + LinkedIn
- 10 stack fit: n8n/dashboard/AI Context OS/Agentforce fit
- 10 NYC/stability fit
- 5 repeatability/IP fit

Threshold:

- 80+ = T1 custom research packet
- 60-79 = T2 light research + contact validation
- 40-59 = market-sensing only
- under 40 = reject
