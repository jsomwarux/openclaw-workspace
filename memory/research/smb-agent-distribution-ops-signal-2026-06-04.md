# SMB Agent Distribution + Ops Signal — 2026-06-04

Purpose: heartbeat client/market research for JT consulting positioning. This is not an outreach list; it is a fresh market signal for how to frame small-business and property-ops AI work.

## Fresh Search Check

Searches used `scripts/web_search.py` with `--freshness week` or `--freshness month` after sourcing `~/.config/env/global.env`.

## What Surfaced

- Meta launched a Business Agent across WhatsApp, Messenger, and Instagram for day-to-day business operations, including customer inquiry handling, product recommendations, appointment booking, and overnight chat summaries. This matters because SMB agent distribution is moving into channels local operators already use, not only enterprise CRM suites.
- Salesforce announced Agentforce marketing-team agents around campaign workflow, content generation, localization, outcomes, and handoffs across Slack/MCP and marketing systems. The useful signal is less "AI writes copy" and more "agents coordinate approved content, customer data, workflow state, and measurable outcomes."
- YC's Cohesive listing describes automated sales and marketing outreach for local businesses and property owners. Combined with Meta's messaging-channel push, this validates the local-service/property-owner ICP as a market where agentic tools are being packaged around field capacity, lead handling, and follow-up.

## JT Move

Frame the consulting offer as **agent-ready operating workflows for local and property-heavy businesses**, not generic chatbot setup.

Useful wedge language:

- Map the intake channel: WhatsApp, Instagram, website, email, forms, phone notes, or property-manager inbox.
- Define what the agent can do: qualify, summarize, route, draft, schedule, collect missing fields, and trigger follow-up.
- Define what stays human-reviewed: pricing, legal notices, vendor approvals, delinquency escalation, sensitive tenant/customer communication, and final send.
- Add proof controls: budget ceilings, approval gates, audit trail, source-of-truth fields, and escalation paths.

Best fit with current lanes:

- **Altmark:** rent delinquency workflow can be shown as the approval-gated version of the same pattern: agent/workflow prepares the action, human gate decides send/deploy.
- **Guyana supplier ops:** local-content suppliers will need intake, document readiness, vendor follow-up, bid/admin reminders, and proof-of-compliance records before they need a flashy AI agent.
- **Local-service outreach:** Cohesive-style automation validates the buyer category, but JT should sell implementation controls and workflow quality rather than competing on pure outbound automation.
