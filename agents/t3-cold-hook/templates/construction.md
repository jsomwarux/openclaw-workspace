# Construction & Skilled Trades — T3 Cold Hook Template

## The Core Pain (for this niche)
Construction companies and contractors burn hours on job coordination that should be automated — dispatching, status updates, job cost tracking, closeout paperwork. Field-to-office communication is fragmented. Project coordinators spend half their day on tasks a workflow could handle.

## Proof Point to Reference
"Built a construction progress tracker and an automated property scraper for a NYC real estate firm — replaced 15–20 hours/week of manual status updates and listing research."

---

## Hook Signal Priority (use first available)
1. A job posting for "project coordinator" or "dispatcher" — signals manual ops pain
2. Google/Yelp reviews mentioning scheduling, communication, or coordination issues
3. ServiceTitan or Jobber badge on website — signals they use field service software (integration opportunity)
4. Company scale/specialty detail (# of employees, project type, service area)

---

## Subject Line Examples (by hook type)
- Job posting hook: "Your next coordinator shouldn't be answering status calls"
- Review hook: "The scheduling gap in your reviews"
- Field service software hook: "What ServiceTitan doesn't do automatically"
- General: "Job closeout without the back-and-forth"

---

## DM Template Structure

**Opener:** [Specific observation about company — job posting, review detail, or scale fact].

**Problem reframe:** Most [contractor type] coordinators spend 40%+ of their day on status calls, job updates, and closeout paperwork — none of which requires a human.

**Proof:** I built a construction progress tracker for a NYC real estate firm that replaced their entire manual update workflow. Their project managers stopped fielding daily "where are we?" calls.

**CTA:** Worth a quick look at how that'd work for [Company]?
