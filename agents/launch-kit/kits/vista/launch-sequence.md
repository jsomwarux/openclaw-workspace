# Vista — 30-Day Launch Sequence

## Overview

Week 1-2: Daily specific actions. Hands-on every day.
Week 3-4: Weekly cadence. Maintain momentum without burning out.

---

## Week 1 — Launch and Seed

**Goal:** Get the first 50 ratings logged in real accounts. Get 10 people who are not JT using Vista.

### Day 1 (Launch Day)
- Post launch announcement on X: "Shipped. Vista is live on the App Store." Include App Store link in bio, not in tweet body.
- Post on LinkedIn: Same message with one extra sentence of professional context.
- Text 5-10 friends personally. Not a group message. Individual texts. "Hey, I shipped the app. Would mean a lot if you downloaded it and rated a few movies." This is the highest-conversion action of the entire launch.
- Do NOT post on Reddit today. Too early.

### Day 2
- Reply to every comment on the Day 1 X post. Even one-word replies.
- Send the demo video to the App Store listing if it's not already there.
- Check App Store Connect for any early crash reports or bugs. Fix before driving more traffic.

### Day 3
- Post the "Why I built this" post on X.
- DM 3 creators from the UGC list (after JT reviews them). Start with the ones most likely to respond: smallest follower counts tend to reply faster.
- Start posting in r/Letterboxd as yourself, not about Vista. Add 3 comments to existing threads with genuine movie opinions.

### Day 4
- Check if any creator has responded. Reply within 2 hours.
- Post your own Vista taste profile on X as an image. Caption: "Two weeks of ratings. This is what showed up." Show the data, not the app. Let the data be the hook.
- Comment on 3 threads in r/movies. No Vista mention.

### Day 5
- Post on r/iphoneapps with the developer launch post (see subreddits.md for strategy). Keep it genuine.
- Add the "What surprised me" post draft to a scheduled queue. Publish in Week 2 after more user data.
- Ask 2-3 early users (friends who downloaded it) for honest feedback. Not a review request. Genuine feedback. Use it.

### Day 6
- Rest from posting. Check metrics. What performed? What got ignored?
- Review App Store analytics in App Store Connect. Note where your first installs came from.
- DM 2 more creators from the list if first batch hasn't responded.

### Day 7
- Post one piece of content based on actual early user data. What are people rating highly? What surprised you about how they used the app? Real data makes better posts than planned content.

---

## Week 2 — Build Momentum

**Goal:** Hit 200 total films rated. Get first organic download (someone who doesn't know JT personally).

### Day 8
- Post "What surprised me" on X with a real data point from the first week.
- Post in r/Letterboxd with the discussion starter post (see subreddits.md). Monitor closely.

### Day 9
- Reply to every comment in the Reddit thread. Do not be defensive. Be curious and engaged.
- Check if any UGC creator posted a video. If yes, share it on X immediately with a thank-you.

### Day 10
- Post one short thread on X: "Things I learned building a movie rating app." 3 tweets. Real observations, not feature announcements.

### Day 11
- Reach out to 2 more creators. Prioritize anyone with active engagement in the last 30 days on their page.
- Engage with 5 posts in #filmtok and #movieapp on TikTok to warm the algorithm before posting there.

### Day 12
- Post the demo video on TikTok. Use the caption from the demo video brief.
- Post on X simultaneously with a link to the TikTok.

### Day 13
- Monitor TikTok video performance. If over 500 views in first 24 hours, engage aggressively with every comment.
- Post a rate-my-taste-profile style engagement post on X: "Rate your last 10 films on a scale of 1-100 in your head. Do your top 3 feel obvious or surprising?" Engagement-first, product mention in reply only.

### Day 14 (End of Week 2)
- Full metrics review. Update the numbers in the metrics section below.
- Decide: Is Reddit working? Is TikTok working? Double down on what moved.

---

## Week 3 — Consolidate

**Goal:** 500 total films rated. First 5-star App Store review.

### Weekly Actions
- 3 posts per week on X minimum. Mix of: product updates, personal film opinions, data observations.
- 1 post on Reddit in a film-adjacent sub. No Vista mention unless invited.
- 1 TikTok video per week. Can reuse the demo video with a new caption if needed.
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours. Always.
- Check App Store Connect weekly for crashes, new reviews, keyword rankings.
- Follow up with any creator who posted but hasn't shared results.

---

## Week 4 — Scale What Works

**Goal:** 1,000 total films rated. Organic growth visible (installs from search, not from JT's social).

### Weekly Actions
- Identify the 1-2 distribution channels that drove the most installs. Focus all energy there.
- Post a "Month 1" recap on X and LinkedIn with real numbers (downloads, films rated, ratings logged).
- Submit to any indie app newsletters or ProductHunt if it hasn't been done yet.
- Begin ASO review: are your keywords returning Vista in search? Test by searching your own keywords from a logged-out Apple account.

---

## Metrics to Watch Weekly

| Metric | Where to find it | Target by Day 30 |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Total installs | App Store Connect | 500+ |
| Active users (weekly) | App Store Connect | 100+ |
| Films rated (total) | App analytics if built | 1,000+ |
| App Store rating | App Store product page | 4.5+ |
| Crash-free session rate | Xcode Organizer / Crashlytics | 99%+ |
| Top traffic source | App Store Connect Analytics | Know your #1 source |

---

## When to Declare Traction

Traction threshold: **Week 3 with consistent organic installs (5+ per day from App Store search, not social referrals) AND at least one user who found Vista without knowing JT.**

Organic search installs are the signal. Friend downloads are noise for traction measurement. When strangers download it because they searched "movie rating app" or "film tracker" and found Vista, that's traction.

Secondary threshold: **First viral piece of content** (any platform, any post) hitting 50,000 impressions with measurable install spike on same day.
