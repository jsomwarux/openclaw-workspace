## Highest ROI Actions (Ranked)

**1. Personal texts to 10 friends on Day 1.**
This is the highest-converting action in the entire kit. Every download from a genuine personal request converts to a real user who will rate films and generate data. It costs nothing and happens in 20 minutes. Do this before posting anything publicly.

**2. ASO subtitle and keyword field update.**
Your App Store metadata drives passive organic installs forever. Changing the subtitle to "Rate, Track & Rank Your Films" and optimizing the keyword field takes 15 minutes in App Store Connect and compounds indefinitely. This is the highest ROI per hour of anything in this kit.

**3. r/Letterboxd discussion post in Week 2.**
This is the one Reddit move with real ceiling. The Letterboxd subreddit is full of exactly the people who will understand and value Vista's 1-100 scale. A post that lands there can drive hundreds of organic clicks. The key is framing it as a genuine discussion, not a promotion. See subreddits.md for the full draft.
