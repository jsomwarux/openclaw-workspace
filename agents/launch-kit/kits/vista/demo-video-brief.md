# Vista — 60-Second Demo Video Brief

## Style Notes

- **Aesthetic:** Cinematic dark mode. Deep black background, warm amber and soft gold accents. No bright whites.
- **Film grain:** Light grain texture overlay throughout (not heavy — tasteful)
- **Tone palette:** Muted warm tones. Think film poster energy, not tech startup SaaS promo.
- **Faceless:** No face on camera. Use screen recordings, stylized text overlays, and hands-on-phone footage at most.
- **Text:** Clean sans-serif font. Minimal. Each word earns its place.
- **Music:** Lo-fi or ambient. No upbeat corporate tracks. Think Nujabes energy or quiet cinematic score.
- **Pacing:** Slow to medium. Let the UI breathe. Don't rush the reveal.
- **Edit style:** Cuts on beat. No jump cuts. One smooth transition between each section.

---

## 60-Second Script

### Hook (0-5s)
**[On screen: nothing. Fade in from black. Text appears slowly.]**

> "You watched 200 movies last year."
> "You remember 12 of them."

**[Cut to phone screen unlocking. Vista app opens.]**

---

### Problem (5-15s)
**[On screen: split visual — Rotten Tomatoes 87% next to IMDb 7.2 side by side.]**

**[Voiceover / text overlay:]**

> "Sites tell you what critics think."
> "Nobody tells you what YOU think."
> "A 4-star and a 5-star look the same when you're trying to rank your actual favorites."

**[Cut to Letterboxd screen showing 4.5 rating. Freeze frame.]**

> "The system isn't built for you."

---

### Solution Reveal (15-40s)
**[Cut to Vista home screen. Warm dark UI. App feels premium.]**

**[Text overlay, one line at a time:]**

> "Vista. 1 to 100."

**[Screen shows rating a movie — thumb dragging slider from 0 to 87. Number gets larger as it moves. Film grain texture visible.]**

> "87 means something different than 90."

**[Cut to taste profile screen. Shows: All Time Favorite, Recent Favorite, Top Genre, Top Performer.]**

> "Your taste profile builds itself."

**[Cut to Friends tab. Feed showing three friends' recent ratings, comments, likes.]**

> "Your friends rate too. You see it all."

**[Cut to Explore tab — trending films, watchlist.]**

> "Your watchlist lives here too."

---

### Social Proof (40-50s)
**[Dark screen. White text fades in.]**

> "Built by one person."
> "Because star ratings stopped working."

**[Cut to App Store rating screenshot — keep real, do not mock up fake reviews.]**

> "On the App Store now."

---

### CTA (50-60s)
**[Fade to black. Vista logo centered on screen.]**

**[Text:]**

> "Download Vista."
> "Find out what you actually think."

**[App Store badge appears below logo. Link in bio text at bottom.]**

**[Hold 3 seconds. Fade out.]**

## Production Notes

- Keep each text overlay on screen for at least 2 seconds. Let it land.
- Do not use the word "download" until the final 10 seconds.
- The taste profile screen is the most important feature reveal. Spend at least 6 seconds there.
- Do not use any green screen. Real phone, real app.
- Shoot at 4K if possible. Export at 1080p for TikTok. 4K for X.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 for TikTok. 1:1 crop available for Instagram. 16:9 for YouTube optional.
