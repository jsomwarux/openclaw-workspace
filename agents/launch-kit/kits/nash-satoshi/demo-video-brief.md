# Demo Video Brief — Nash Satoshi

## Format
Faceless slideshow. Dark/navy background, white text, minimal data-forward visuals. No on-camera. Consistent font across all slides. Data aesthetic — think Bloomberg terminal energy, not crypto influencer energy.

---

## Hook (0-5 seconds)
**Slide text:** "Most crypto rankings are just price momentum with extra steps."

Alternative: "4 AI models. 1 question: which crypto assets have game-theoretically coherent incentive structures?"

*Goal: Stop the scroll with a contrarian claim that doesn't require prior knowledge of Nash Satoshi.*

---

## Problem (5-15 seconds)
**Slide text:** "Price tells you what the market thinks right now. It tells you nothing about whether a protocol's incentive architecture is stable — or whether participants are about to defect."

Voiceover (optional): "Every ranking you've seen is momentum-based. Buy signal, sell signal, sentiment score. None of them ask the structural question."

---

## Solution reveal (15-40 seconds)
**Show:** The Nash Satoshi site — rankings page, a specific asset score, the methodology summary.

**Slide text overlay:** "Nash Satoshi runs every asset through a 4-LLM ensemble. Each model scores independently. Game theory dimensions only — no price, no sentiment."

Key details to show on screen:
- The ranking table (top 10 assets visible)
- One asset's score breakdown if visible on site
- The methodology note explaining "4 LLMs, independent evaluation, aggregated score"

**Do NOT show:** Price predictions, return estimates, "buy this asset" language anywhere.

---

## Social proof (40-50 seconds)
**Slide text:** "Built by JT Somwaru, AI automation consultant. Live at nashsatoshi.com."

Or: "Real system. Live rankings. No price targets."

*Keep it minimal. The credibility is in the methodology, not a follower count.*

---

## CTA (50-60 seconds)
**Slide text:** "Full rankings at nashsatoshi.com — link in bio."

Keep it simple. One CTA. No urgency framing ("act now," "before it's too late" — never).

---

## Style notes
- Dark navy or black background every slide
- White or light gray text only
- No red/green color coding (too much like trading signals)
- Font: clean sans-serif. Consistent across all slides.
- No hype emojis in the video itself (🚀🔥💯 — zero tolerance)
- Pacing: 2-3 seconds per slide minimum. Not frenetic.
- Music if any: lo-fi or ambient. Nothing aggressive.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 for TikTok. Export at 1080x1920.
