# Nash Satoshi — Product

## What it is

Nash Satoshi ranks crypto projects through a game theory lens using a 4-LLM ensemble model. Instead of price momentum or sentiment, it evaluates projects on structural positioning: incentive design, coordination dynamics, rational actor behavior, and competitive equilibrium.

## The core insight

Most crypto analysis is sentiment or momentum-based. Nash Satoshi asks a different question: given the incentive structures in place, what do rational actors do next? That's a game theory question, not a price prediction.

## What it produces

Rankings updated regularly. Each project scored on game theory positioning — not a price target, not a "buy" signal. A structural assessment.

## How it works

4 LLMs evaluate each project independently across game theory dimensions. Results are aggregated into a composite score. Human-verified before publishing.

## What it doesn't do

- It does NOT predict price
- It does NOT guarantee returns
- It does NOT replace your own research

Any post implying otherwise violates the brand. No exceptions.
