# Vista — Product

## What it is

Vista is a movie rating app on the App Store. You rate movies 1–100, build a taste profile over time, and see how your ratings compare with friends.

## Core features (confirmed — only reference these)

- 1–100 rating scale (more granular than 5-star)
- Personal taste profile (All Time Favorite, Recent Favorite, Top Genre, Top Performer)
- Friends tab — see friends' ratings, like and comment (Instagram-style feed)
- Explore tab — trending movies + watchlist
- Rating history — all your ratings in one place

## The core insight

Crowdsourced ratings (IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes) average across millions of people with wildly different tastes. A 7.2 IMDb score tells you almost nothing about whether *you* will like the film. Your own rating history — with enough data — is a much stronger signal for your taste.

## What it doesn't do

- It is NOT an AI recommendation engine (don't imply automated recommendations unless confirmed)
- It is NOT affiliated with Letterboxd, IMDb, or Rotten Tomatoes
- It does NOT have a web version (App Store only)
