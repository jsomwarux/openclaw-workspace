Most AI-crypto content evaluates whether the story sounds plausible. Nash Satoshi should ask whether participants have a reason to keep the system honest after attention fades.

## Angle 2: The exit incentive is the missing metric
Crypto rankings over-index on upside narratives. A stronger frame is: who is incentivized to leave first, and what happens when they do?

## Angle 4: Coordination beats conviction
People talk about conviction like it is edge. Nash Satoshi should frame durable crypto strength as coordination under pressure.

## Angle 5: A good token can still be a bad game
Separate product quality from incentive design. This is the most reusable contrarian angle for X and Reddit.
