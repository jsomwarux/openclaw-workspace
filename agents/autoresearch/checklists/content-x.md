# Autoresearch Checklist — content-generate-x (cron)
# X post scoring (yes=1, no=0, averaged across test inputs)

1. Does the first line open with the point — no warmup, no preamble?
2. Is there no external link in the post body?
3. Is the post free of hashtags?
4. For Thursday hot-take/debate posts: is the claim specific enough that only someone who runs autonomous agents or consulting work would write it (not a generic AI take)?
5. For build-showcase posts: does the final line describe what the build does (capability proof) — not advice for the reader?
6. Is the post free of em dashes and banned adverbs?
