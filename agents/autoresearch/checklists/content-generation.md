# Autoresearch Checklist — content-generation

Score generated X/LinkedIn content against these yes/no checks:

1. Does the draft start with the point instead of a preamble?
2. Does it choose an exact lane from `memory/content/current-niche-map.md`, with LinkedIn defaulting to Tier 1/Tier 2 unless the request explicitly asks for a product/app lane?
3. Does it follow platform constraints for the requested channel (X compression/thread rules or LinkedIn depth/structure)?
4. Does it avoid banned voice patterns and low-value topics: em dashes, hype, generic AI claims, filler openings, internal content ops, and standalone public-proof/privacy cleanup?
5. Does it include at least one concrete proof point, number, client/build, workflow detail, buyer problem, or named niche signal instead of generic personal branding or internal hygiene?
6. Is there a clear next action: post, bank, upload, calendar push, or revise?
