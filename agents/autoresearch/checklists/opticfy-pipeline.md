# Autoresearch Checklist — opticfy-pipeline

Score pipeline outputs against these yes/no checks:

1. Does prospect research identify a specific operational pain, not just company facts?
2. Does the analysis map pain to one practical AI/n8n/Agentforce workflow?
3. Does the deck/demo/outreach angle fit the prospect's niche and maturity?
4. Does it avoid generic AI consultant language?
5. Does it produce review-ready deliverables and Drive links where required?
6. Does it prevent duplicate or unsendable outreach tasks when buyer channel data is missing?
