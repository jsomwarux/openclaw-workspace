# Autoresearch Checklist — jt-consulting-pipeline

Score consulting pipeline outputs with yes/no answers:

1. Does it start from concrete prospect research instead of generic ICP assumptions?
2. Does it identify a specific operational bottleneck, buyer pain, and measurable business outcome?
3. Does it choose the right delivery lane: research-only, deck, demo, outreach draft, or stop/no-fit?
4. Does any demo or automation idea stay feasible for JT's stack and avoid overbuilding/speculative AI transformation?
5. Does outreach/deck copy use JT's practical AI implementation positioning and avoid developer-for-hire or hype framing?
6. Does it save/update the required client folder artifacts and Drive sync instructions without sending outreach externally?
