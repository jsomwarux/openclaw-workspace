# Autoresearch Checklist — job-application
*Skill: skills/job-application/SKILL.md*
*Enrolled: 2026-03-22 | Weekly synthesis*

## Evaluation Questions (yes/no per output)

1. Is the resume output a properly formatted .docx file (NOT markdown, NOT plain text)?
2. Does the resume pass the "no hands-on coding" filter — no Apex, SFDX, Python proficiency framed as primary skill?
3. Is the cover letter opening focused on value delivered to the employer — NOT "I am excited about this role"?
4. Does the resume lead with consulting/implementation accomplishments (quantified where possible) rather than job duties?
5. Were both files uploaded to Google Drive immediately after generation (not saved locally only)?
6. Does the job score meet the 20+/25 threshold and pass hard filters before generating docs?
