# Autoresearch Checklist — job-application
*Skill: skills/job-application/SKILL.md*
*Enrolled: 2026-03-22 | Weekly synthesis*

## Evaluation Questions (yes/no per output)

1. Is the resume output a properly formatted .docx file (NOT markdown, NOT plain text)?
2. Does the resume pass the "no hands-on coding" filter — no Apex, SFDX, Python proficiency framed as primary skill?
3. Is the cover letter opening focused on value delivered to the employer, NOT "I am excited about this role," and free of em dashes?
4. Does the resume lead with consulting/implementation accomplishments (quantified where possible) rather than job duties, and stay free of em dashes?
5. Were both files uploaded to Google Drive immediately after generation (not saved locally only)?
6. Before generating docs, did the role pass hard filters, meet the score threshold, and have live UI/ATS evidence that applications are still accepted (not cached JD text or HTTP 200 alone)?
